Peanut Punch is delectable watery cocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 30ml peanut pulp and 10ml peanut syrup with 15ml condensed milk as well as 30ml coconut cream including 65ml milk also 90ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just put ice into blender then add all ingredients to cocktail blender and blend until silky smooth. Pour blended mixture into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
