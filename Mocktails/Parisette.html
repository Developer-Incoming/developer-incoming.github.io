Parisette is luscious watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 30ml grenadine and 70ml milk with 190ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and add more ice to glass and garnish glass with marcaschino cherries and served in a highball glass cold.
