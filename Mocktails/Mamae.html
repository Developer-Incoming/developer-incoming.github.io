Method - Stirred - Room Temperature
  1. Place water into mixing glass.
  2. Stir in the kava.
  3. Add in the rice milk and stir well.
  4. Slice a little chilli and add to glass.
  5. Add in the ice and shake well.
  6. Stir in sode water.
  7. Hawthorne strain into glass.
  8. ALERT: Don't consume Karva with alcohol.
  9. Garnish top with chili.
  10. Succulent

Mamae is succulent watery mocktail with no alcohol. Stirred with 15ml kava powder kalm borongoru and 15ml water with 30ml soda water as well as 30ml rice milk including 100ml ice and best served with fish and seafood dishes. Just place water into mixing glass then stir in the kava and add in the rice milk and stir well. Slice a little chilli and add to glass and add in the ice and shake well and stir in sode water then hawthorne strain into glass and alert: don't consume karva with alcohol and garnish top with chili and served in a martini glass at room temperature.
