Shirleys Sister is dainty watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 45ml lemon soda and 10ml grenadine with 45ml ice cream strawberry as well as 10ml chocolate syrup including 180ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to glass then pour ingredients over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
