Strawberry Banna Split is beguiling watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 30ml strawberry pulp and 20ml strawberry syrup with 20ml banana pulp as well as 10ml banana syrup monin including 10ml lemon juice also 30ml lemon soda together with 120ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
