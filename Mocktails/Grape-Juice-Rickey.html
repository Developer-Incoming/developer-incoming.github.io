Grape Juice Rickey is flavourful watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 30ml grape juice red and 15ml lime juice with 55ml soda water as well as 190ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just pack ice into glass then pour ingredients over ice and garnish with lime peel knot and served in a highball glass cold.
