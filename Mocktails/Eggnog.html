Eggnog is restoring christmas egg nog watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 140ml milk hot and 60ml cream hot with 60ml egg as well as 20ml sugar including 10ml vanilla essence and best with morning tea snacks. Just add all ingredients to shaker then shake well and strain into glass and garnish with cinnamon for a classic christmas cocktail and served in a coffee glass warm.
