Old Chaas is succulent watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 60ml yoghurt and 60ml water with 30ml lemon juice as well as 30ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add ice into blender then add all ingredients to blender and a pinch of salt and blend until smooth. Pour blended mixture into glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
