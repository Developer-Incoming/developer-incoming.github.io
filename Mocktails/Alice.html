Alice is tasty watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 30ml orange juice and 30ml pineapple juice with 10ml grenadine crawleys as well as 40ml cream including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
