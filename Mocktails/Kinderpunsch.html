Kinderpunsch is heavenly watery mocktail with no alcohol. Stirred with 30ml grape juice white and 30ml grape juice red with 30ml orange juice as well as 10ml sugar syrup spiced including 30ml tea and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just brew tea then warm glass and warm mixing glass and heat cubes. Add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well until mixed and pour into warm glass and served in a sherry glass warm.
