Pina Colada No Alcohol is delectable watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 30ml pineapple juice and 30ml non alcoholic spirit with 30ml coconut cream as well as 20ml pineapple syrup including 100ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients and shake well. Strain into glass and garnish with chopped pineapple and served in a saucer glass cold.
