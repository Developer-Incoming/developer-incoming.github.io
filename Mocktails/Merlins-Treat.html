Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Place ice into shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker, less soda.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Add ginger soda.

Merlins Treat is moreish watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 30ml mango juice and 30ml pineapple juice with 30ml orange juice as well as 10ml strawberry syrup including 30ml ginger ale soda also 190ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then place ice into shaker and add all ingredients to shaker, less soda. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and add ginger soda and served in a goblet glass cold.
