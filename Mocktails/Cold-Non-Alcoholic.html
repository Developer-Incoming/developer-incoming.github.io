Cold Non Alcoholic is rejuvenating watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 100ml water and 190ml ice and good with roasted or barbecue foods. Just chill glass then fill glass with ice and add one drop of orange juice to glass. Add one drop of lemon juice to glass and add one drop of lime juice to glass and top with water and served in a highball glass cold.
