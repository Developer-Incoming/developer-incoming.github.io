Apple Of My Eye is refreshing watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 60ml apple juice and 30ml blackcurrant syrup with 30ml cream as well as 120ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend rough. Pour into glass and garnish with apple and a few black currants and served in a poco grande glass cold.
