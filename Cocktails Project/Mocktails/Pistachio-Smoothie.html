Pistachio Smoothie is flavoursome watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 30ml pistachio syrup giffard and 60ml ice cream pistachio with 30ml cream as well as 20ml milk including 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and garnish with pistachio praline and served in a poco grande glass cold.
