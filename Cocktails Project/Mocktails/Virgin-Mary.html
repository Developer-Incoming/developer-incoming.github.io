Virgin Mary is nummy bloody mary watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 80ml tomato juice and 10ml tabasco pepper sauce with 10ml worcestershire sauce as well as 10ml lemon juice including 190ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add ice to glass then add all ingredients and finish with a stick of celery and served in a highball glass cold.
