Seafood Prairie Oyster is moreish hair of the dog watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 20ml oyster and 30ml egg yolk with 10ml worcestershire sauce as well as 10ml tabasco pepper sauce and best served with fish and seafood dishes. Just add oyster to glass then add worcestershire sauce to glass and add tabasco. Drop in whole egg yolk and drink in one go and season top with salt white pepper and lemon juice and served in a sherry glass warm.
