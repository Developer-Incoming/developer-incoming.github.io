Elixir De Menthe is wisselwerking watery mocktail with no alcohol. Stirred with 20ml honey and 10ml peppermint syrup torani with 120ml water hot and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just warm serving glass then warm mixing glass and heat cubes and add in honey and syrup. Place peppermint leaves into mixing glass and quick shake and stir in hot water then fine strain into glass and garnish with mint and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
