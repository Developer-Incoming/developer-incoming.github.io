Mickey Mouse is divine watery mocktail with no alcohol. Layered with 130ml cola soda and 30ml ice cream with 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice to glass then then cola and add ice-cream and top with whipped cream and maraschino cherry and served in a highball glass cold.
