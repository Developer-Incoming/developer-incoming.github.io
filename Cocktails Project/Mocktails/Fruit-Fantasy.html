Fruit Fantasy is flavoursome watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 30ml orange juice and 30ml pineapple pulp with 10ml kiwi syrup as well as 30ml strawberry pulp including 20ml melon also 120ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and garnish with melon and yellow kiwi fruit and served in a poco grande glass cold.
