Bingo is invigorating watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 20ml papaya juice and 20ml orange juice with 20ml coconut cream as well as 10ml pineapple juice including 10ml lime juice also 30ml strawberry pulp together with 10ml grenadine cawseys and 120ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
