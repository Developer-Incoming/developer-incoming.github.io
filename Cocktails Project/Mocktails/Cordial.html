Cordial is scrumptious watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 30ml orange juice and 30ml mango juice with 30ml lemonade juice as well as 30ml lime juice including 30ml pineapple juice also 20ml sugar syrup together with 130ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just add ice to glass then add all ingredients to shaker and dry shake without ice. Pour into glass over ice and garnish with mixed fruit and served in a goblet glass cold.
