Palermo Amarino is fortifying watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 30ml palermo amarino and 10ml lemon juice with 60ml soda water as well as 190ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just fill glass with ice then add amarino palermo to glass and pour in lemon juice. Top with soda water and garnish with lemon and served in a highball glass cold.
