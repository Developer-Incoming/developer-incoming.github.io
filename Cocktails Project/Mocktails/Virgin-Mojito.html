Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Cut lime into wedges and add to glass.
  2. Add sugar and lime juice to glass.
  3. Also add a little mint.
  4. Muddle well.
  5. Add ice to glass.
  6. Top with soda water.
  7. Garnish with mint.

Virgin Mojito is energizing watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 30ml lime juice and 15ml lime syrup with 45ml soda water as well as 10ml sugar including 190ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just cut lime into wedges and add to glass then add sugar and lime juice to glass and also add a little mint. Muddle well and add ice to glass and top with soda water and garnish with mint and served in a highball glass cold.
