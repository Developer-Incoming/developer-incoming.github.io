Peach Magnifico is succulent watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 30ml peach juice and 30ml mango juice with 40ml ginger ale soda as well as 190ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then pour ingredients over ice and top with ginger ale soda and served in a highball glass cold.
