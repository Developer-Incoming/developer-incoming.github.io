Boo Boos Special is very enjoyable watered down mocktail at 0.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 40ml pineapple juice and 40ml orange juice with 10ml lemon juice as well as 5ml bitters blood orange including 5ml grenadine and best with lunch. Just add ice to shaker then shake well and strain into glass and garnish with fruit and served in a flute glass cold.
