Ugly Virgin is dainty watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 30ml tangerine juice and 30ml grapefruit juice with 50ml lemon soda as well as 190ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just pack ice into glass then pour ingredients over ice and top with lemon soda and garnish with candied tangerine and served in a highball glass cold.
