Orgeat Lemonade is tart watery mocktail with no alcohol. Built with 30ml lemonade juice and 15ml orgeat syrup with 125ml soda water as well as 130ml ice and best with lunch. Just place ice into glass then add lemonade juice to glass and pour in orgeat syrup. Add soda water and stir well and slice thin rounds and cut in quarters and served in a goblet glass cold.
