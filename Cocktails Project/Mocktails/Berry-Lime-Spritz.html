Berry Lime Spritz is scrummy watery mocktail with no alcohol. Stirred with 60ml palermo bianco and 20ml lime juice with 20ml lemon juice as well as 70ml soda water including 130ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just add ice to glass then add slices of lime and lemon and add raspberries to glass. Pour in palermo bianco and pour in juices and add soda water then stir a little and served in a goblet glass cold.
