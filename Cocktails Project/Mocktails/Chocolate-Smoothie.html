Chocolate Smoothie is delicious watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 10ml chocolate and 20ml chocolate syrup monin with 10ml cocoa powder as well as 60ml ice cream chocolate including 30ml cream also 110ml ice and best with dessert type dishes.
