Chaas is delectable watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 60ml yoghurt and 60ml camel milk with 30ml water as well as 30ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just put ice into blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour blended mixture into glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
