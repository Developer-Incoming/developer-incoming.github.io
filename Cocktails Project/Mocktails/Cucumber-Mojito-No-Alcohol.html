Cucumber Mojito No Alcohol is invigorating watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 15ml rum light white non alcoholic and 15ml non alcoholic spirit with 15ml lime juice as well as 10ml cucumber syrup including 15ml cucumber juice also 30ml soda water together with 190ml ice and best at tea time. Just add half the ice to shaker then add half the mint to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker, less soda. Vigorously shake and pour all into glass and top glass with ice and soda water and garnish with cucumber and mint and served in a highball glass cold.
