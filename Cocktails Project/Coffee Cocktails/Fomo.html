Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur
30 ml
Chocolate Liqueur Brown BVLand
30 ml
Cream
10 ml
Vanilla Liqueur
10 ml
Caramel Syrup
100 ml
Ice
15% Alcohol By Volume
30 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Pour in coffee and chocolate liqueurs.
  3. Quick shake.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Add cream vanilla liqueur and caramel syrup.
  6. Shake well until fluffy and foamy.
  7. Float on top.
  8. Very Enjoyable

Fomo: Fear of Missing Out

Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl
Also See: Fear Of Missing Out Former Mormon

Fomo is very enjoyable full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur and 30ml chocolate liqueur brown bvland with 30ml cream as well as 10ml vanilla liqueur including 10ml caramel syrup also 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice to shaker then pour in coffee and chocolate liqueurs and quick shake. Strain into glass and add cream vanilla liqueur and caramel syrup and shake well until fluffy and foamy then float on top and served in a saucer glass cold.
