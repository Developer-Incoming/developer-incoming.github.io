Jailbreak is good looking above average cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Built with 30ml bourbon and 15ml coffee liqueur with 15ml almond liqueur as well as 55ml beer brew stout including 55ml cola soda also 130ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice into glass then pour in shot of bourbon and add coffee and almond liqueur. Pour in cola soda and add stout beer and served in a goblet glass cold.
