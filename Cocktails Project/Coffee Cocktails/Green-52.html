Ingredients
30 ml
Whisky Cream
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Meukow
20 ml
Absinthe Earp
Drinking Straw
29% Alcohol By Volume
58 Proof
1.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Room Temperature
  1. Place whisky cream into glass.
  2. Add coffee liquer.
  3. Float absinthe on top.
  4. Ignite alcohol on top.
  5. Drink quickly with straw.

Served On Fire
Layered
Room Temperature

90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Green 52 is luscious very strong cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml whisky cream and 30ml coffee liqueur meukow with 20ml absinthe earp and best with buffet style food. Just place whisky cream into glass then add coffee liquer and float absinthe on top. Ignite alcohol on top and drink quickly with straw and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
