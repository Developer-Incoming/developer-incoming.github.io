After Eight Shot is delectable strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur kahlua and 20ml peppermint liqueur green with 20ml whisky cream irish baileys and best with buffet style food. Just two shots always share a shot then pour in coffee liqueur and float peppermint liqueur. Float whiskey cream on top and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
