Ingredients
45 ml
Bourbon Jack Daniels
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Kahlua Mocha
10 ml
Vanilla Syrup
100 ml
Ice
Salt
30% Alcohol By Volume
60 Proof
1.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Place pinch of salt into shaker.
  3. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  4. Quick shake.
  5. Pour everything into glass.
  6. Top glass with ice.
  7. Schmackhaft
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Jack Saylor is schmackhaft very strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml bourbon jack daniels and 15ml coffee liqueur kahlua mocha with 10ml vanilla syrup as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then place pinch of salt into shaker and add ingredients into shaker over ice. Quick shake and pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
