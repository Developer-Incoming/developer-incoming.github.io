Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur After Supreme
30 ml
Rum Coconut Admiral Nelson
Whisky Cream Irish Feeneys Luxurious
31% Alcohol By Volume
62 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Room Temperature
  1. TWO SHOTS - Always Share A Shot :)
  2. Warm two shot glass.
  3. Pour coffee liqueur into glass.
  4. Float coconut rum on top.
  5. Add drop of whisky cream to each glass.
  6. Energizing
Served
Layered
Room Temperature
Shot Glass
30ml, 1oz, 3cl

Shot In The Dark is energizing heavy cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml coffee liqueur after supreme and 30ml rum coconut admiral nelson and best with an entree or small snack. Just two shots always share a shot then warm two shot glass and pour coffee liqueur into glass. Float coconut rum on top and add drop of whisky cream to each glass and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
