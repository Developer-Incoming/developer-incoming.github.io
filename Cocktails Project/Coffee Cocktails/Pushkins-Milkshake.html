Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Kavalan
30 ml
Orange Liqueur
10 ml
Chocolate Syrup
30 ml
Milk
190 ml
Ice
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Add more ice to glass.
  6. Succulent
Served
Shaken
Cold
Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Pushkins Milkshake is succulent average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur kavalan and 30ml orange liqueur with 10ml chocolate syrup as well as 30ml milk including 190ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Pour all into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a highball glass cold.
