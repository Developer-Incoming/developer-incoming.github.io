Bitch Bloody is heavenly quite strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Built with 15ml coffee liqueur and 15ml chocolate liqueur with 15ml rum as well as 15ml raspberry liqueur including 15ml butterscotch liqueur also 15ml grenadine together with 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Pour all into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
