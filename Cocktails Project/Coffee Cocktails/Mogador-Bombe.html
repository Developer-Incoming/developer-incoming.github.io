Mogador Bombe is comestible full bodied cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 60ml maraschino liqueur and 50ml ice cream coffee and served as or with a sorbet course. Just chill glass then add scoop of coffee ice cream to glass and pour over maraschino liqueur and garnsih with maraschino cherries and served in a saucer glass cold.
