Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Misceo
30 ml
Coffee Brew
60 ml
Milk Hot
30 ml
Cream Whipped
7% Alcohol By Volume
14 Proof
0.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee and stream milk.
  3. Add brewed coffee into glass.
  4. Pour in shot of coffee liqueur.
  5. Pour in warm milk.
  6. Add whipped cream on top.
  7. Finger Licking
Served
Poured
Warm
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Viennois is finger licking mild cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur misceo and 30ml coffee brew with 60ml milk hot as well as 30ml cream whipped and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and stream milk and add brewed coffee into glass. Pour in shot of coffee liqueur and pour in warm milk and add whipped cream on top and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
