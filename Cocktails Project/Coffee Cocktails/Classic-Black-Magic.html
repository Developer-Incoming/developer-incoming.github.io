Ingredients
45 ml
Vodka Luksusowa
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Merlet
10 ml
Lime Juice
200 ml
Ice
33% Alcohol By Volume
66 Proof
1.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Fill glass with ice to chill.
  2. Place ice into shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Nectareous
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl
Also See: Black Magic

Classic Black Magic is nectareous heavy cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml vodka luksusowa and 15ml coffee liqueur merlet with 10ml lime juice as well as 200ml ice and works well with savoury style foods. Just fill glass with ice to chill then place ice into shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
