Ingredients
30 ml
Peach Schnapps
30 ml
Vodka Coffee Whisky Jumping Goat
30 ml
Peach Juice
100 ml
Ice
Carambola
18% Alcohol By Volume
36 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Place ice into mixing glass.
  2. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  3. Stir well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Garnish with carambola fruit cut into a star slice.
  6. Lovely
Served
Stirred
Cold
Martini Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Twinkle Twinkle is lovely strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml peach schnapps and 30ml vodka coffee whisky jumping goat with 30ml peach juice as well as 100ml ice and had with salad dishes. Just place ice into mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and garnish with carambola fruit cut into a star slice and served in a martini glass cold.
