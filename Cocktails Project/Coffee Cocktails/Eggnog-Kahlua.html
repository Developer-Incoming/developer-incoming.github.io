Ingredients
60 ml
Advocaat Spirit49
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Kahlua
100 ml
Ice
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Vigorously shake.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Add more ice to glass.
  6. Reviving
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Eggnog Kahlua is reviving quite strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 60ml advocaat spirit49 and 30ml coffee liqueur kahlua with 100ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Pour everything into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
