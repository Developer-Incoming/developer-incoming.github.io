Black Magic is freshening strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml rum dark and 15ml coffee liqueur kaffe arabika with 30ml mango liqueur briottet as well as 15ml pineapple juice including 10ml lemon juice also 10ml orange juice together with 10ml molasses and 120ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
