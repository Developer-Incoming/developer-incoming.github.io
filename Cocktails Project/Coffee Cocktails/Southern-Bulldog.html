Southern Bulldog is flavourful above average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 45ml pecan liqueur and 15ml coffee liqueur with 40ml cola soda as well as 190ml ice and best with buffet style food. Just add ice to glass then add pecan liqueuer to glass and add coffee liqueuer. Top with cola soda and served in a highball glass cold.
