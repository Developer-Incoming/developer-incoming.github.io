Midnight Madness is delicious very strong cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml orange liqueur and 30ml brandy with 30ml coffee liqueur and best with lunch. Just pour orange liqueur into bottom of glass then float brandy on top and float coffee liquer and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
