Choc Mint Expresso Martini is palatable very strong cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Stirred with 45ml gin dry jensens bermondsey and 15ml chocolate liqueur tamborine mountain with 15ml peppermint liqueur as well as 15ml coffee brew including 100ml ice and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and garnish with peppermint leaf and served in a martini glass cold.
