Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Steam milk and brew coffee.
  3. Add coffee liqueur to glass.
  4. Add brewed coffee to glass.
  5. Pour in steamed milk.
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.

Cafe Con Leche is delicioso light in flavour cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur misceo and 30ml coffee brew with 190ml milk hot and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then steam milk and brew coffee and add coffee liqueur to glass. Add brewed coffee to glass and pour in steamed milk and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
