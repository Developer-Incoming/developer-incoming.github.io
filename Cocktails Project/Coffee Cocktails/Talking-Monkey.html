Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Brew and chill coffee.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Quick shake.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Flick a little chocolate powder on top.
  7. Moreish

120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Talking Monkey is moreish strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml vodka and 15ml chocolate liqueur with 15ml banana liqueur as well as 30ml cream including 10ml coffee brew and best with buffet style food. Just brew and chill coffee then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Quick shake and strain into glass and flick a little chocolate powder on top and served in a saucer glass cold.
