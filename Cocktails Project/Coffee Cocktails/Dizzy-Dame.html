Ingredients
30 ml
Brandy Grape Armagnac Chabot
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Mr Black Amaro
15 ml
Brandy Cherry Kirschwasser
20 ml
Cream
20 ml
Ice
27% Alcohol By Volume
54 Proof
1.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Add ice to blender.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into glass.
  5. Mouth Watering
Served
Blended
Cold
Flute Glass
150ml, 5oz, 15cl

Dizzy Dame is mouth watering very strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml brandy grape armagnac chabot and 15ml coffee liqueur mr black amaro with 15ml brandy cherry kirschwasser as well as 20ml cream including 20ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and served in a flute glass cold.
