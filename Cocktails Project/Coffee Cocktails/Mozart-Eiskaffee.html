Ingredients
60 ml
Chocolate Liqueur Mozart
20 ml
Coffee Brew
20 ml
Ice Cream Vanilla
120 ml
Ice
20 ml
Cream Whipped
8% Alcohol By Volume
16 Proof
0.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Brew coffee and chill.
  2. Add ice to blender.
  3. Add all ingredients to blender, less whipped cream.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour into glass.
  6. Top with whipped cream.
  7. Scrummy
Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Mozart Eiskaffee is scrummy smooth cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Blended with 60ml chocolate liqueur mozart and 20ml coffee brew with 20ml ice cream vanilla as well as 120ml ice including 20ml cream whipped and served with sweet dishes. Just brew coffee and chill then add ice to blender and add all ingredients to blender, less whipped cream. Blend until smooth and pour into glass and top with whipped cream and served in a poco grande glass cold.
