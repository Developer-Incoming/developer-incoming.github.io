Raging Bull is beguiling very strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Built with 30ml tequila reposado avion and 30ml coffee liqueur kahlua with 10ml sambuca bottega and best with an entree or small snack. Just add gold tequila to glass then float coffee liqueur on tequila and float and light sambuca on top and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
