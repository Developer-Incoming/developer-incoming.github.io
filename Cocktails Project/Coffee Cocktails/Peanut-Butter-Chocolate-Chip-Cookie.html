Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie is reviving full bodied cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml hazelnut liqueur and 15ml coffee liqueur cayman reef with 15ml peanut liqueur as well as 15ml milk and served for or with tiffins. Just add some ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and quick shake to mix. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and serve chocolate chip cookie on the side and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
