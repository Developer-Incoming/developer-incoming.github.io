Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well until well mixed and emulsified.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Garnish with gold and yellow dust.
  6. Tasty

Butter Mocha Flip is tasty standard cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml butterscotch schnapps vok and 15ml coffee schnapps with 30ml chocolate schnapps as well as 30ml egg yolk including 20ml milk also 100ml ice and best with egg dishes. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well until well mixed and emulsified. Strain into glass and garnish with gold and yellow dust and served in a saucer glass cold.
