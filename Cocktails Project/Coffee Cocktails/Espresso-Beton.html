Espresso Beton is inspiriting average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml becherovka and 30ml coffee brew with 40ml tonic water as well as 190ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just brew and chill coffee then pack ice into glass and add becherovka liqueur. Add tonic water and drizzle cold brewed coffee on top and served in a highball glass cold.
