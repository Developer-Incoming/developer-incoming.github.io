Magic Mojo Mix is delicioso strong cocktail at 2.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml tequila anejo la cofradia and 30ml vodka black teichenne with 30ml coffee liqueur as well as 20ml lime juice including 20ml lemon juice also 20ml pineapple juice together with 20ml orange juice and 130ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice to glass then pour in vodka and tequila and stir in juices. Sprinkle coffee liqueur on top and served in a goblet glass cold.
