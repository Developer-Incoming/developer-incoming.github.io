Amalyser is rejuvenating full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Stirred with 20ml vodka skillion blue and 20ml amarula cream with 20ml coffee liqueur as well as 40ml cola soda including 190ml ice and best at tea time. Just fill glass with ice then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Pour into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
