  2. Add ice to mixing glass.

Pumpkin Espresso Martini is scrummy strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml vodka summer land and 30ml pumpkin liqueur mozart with 30ml coffee brew as well as 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just brew and chill coffee then add ice to mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Stir ingredients together until mixed and hawthorne strain into glass and served in a martini glass cold.
