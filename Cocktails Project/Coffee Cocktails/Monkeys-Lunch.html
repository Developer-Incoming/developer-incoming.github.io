Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.

Served

Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Monkeys Lunch is delectable full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur manly spirits and 30ml banana liqueur with 30ml milk as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
