Ingredients
15 ml
Chocolate Liqueur Baitz
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Galliano
30 ml
Vodka Petrovska
100 ml
Ice
32% Alcohol By Volume
64 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Palatable
Served
Stirred
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Iced Mocha Tini is palatable heavy cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml chocolate liqueur baitz and 15ml coffee liqueur galliano with 30ml vodka petrovska as well as 100ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
