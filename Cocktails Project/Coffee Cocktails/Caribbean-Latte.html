Ingredients
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Ambra
15 ml
Rum Coconut Bacardi
15 ml
Coffee Brew
55 ml
Milk
190 ml
Ice
10% Alcohol By Volume
20 Proof
0.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to glass.
  2. Brew strong coffee and chill.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Dry shake without ice.
  5. Pour into glass over ice.
  6. Stimulating
Served
Shaken
Cold
Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Caribbean Latte is stimulating standard cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml coffee liqueur ambra and 15ml rum coconut bacardi with 15ml coffee brew as well as 55ml milk including 190ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just add ice to glass then brew strong coffee and chill and add all ingredients to shaker. Dry shake without ice and pour into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
