Rum ALa Olof Shot is flavoursome extremely strong cocktail at 2.3 standard drinks. Built with 30ml rum stroh 80 and 10ml lemon juice with 10ml coffee liqueur as well as 10ml chocolate liqueur brown and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just two shots always share a shot then add a little lemon juice into glass and pour in half the strong stroh rum. Add coffe and chocolate liqueur and top with whipped cream on top and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
