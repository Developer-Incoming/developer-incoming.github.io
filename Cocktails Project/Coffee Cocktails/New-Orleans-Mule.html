New Orleans Mule is schmackhaft full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Built with 30ml bourbon and 15ml coffee liqueur with 55ml ginger ale soda as well as 190ml ice and best at tea time. Just put the ice into glass then add bourbon into glass and add ginger ale. Flaot coffee liqueur on top and served in a highball glass cold.
