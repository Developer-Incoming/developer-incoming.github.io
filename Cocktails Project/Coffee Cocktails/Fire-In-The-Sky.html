Ingredients
30 ml
Melon Liqueur
30 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur
15 ml
Strawberry Liqueur
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur
10 ml
Grenadine
20 ml
Cream
Chocolate Shavings
16% Alcohol By Volume
32 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Cold
  1. Add grenadine into bottom of glass.
  2. Pour melon liqueur into bottom of glass.
  3. Float in hazelnut liqueur.
  4. Layer strawberry liqueur.
  5. Layer coffee liqueur
  6. Float cream on top.
  7. Sprinkle chocolate shavings on top.
  8. Dainty
Served
Layered
Cold
Martini Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Fire In The Sky is dainty full bodied cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml melon liqueur and 30ml hazelnut liqueur with 15ml strawberry liqueur as well as 15ml coffee liqueur including 10ml grenadine also 20ml cream and best with lunch. Just add grenadine into bottom of glass then pour melon liqueur into bottom of glass and float in hazelnut liqueur. Layer strawberry liqueur and layer coffee liqueur and float cream on top and sprinkle chocolate shavings on top and served in a martini glass cold.
