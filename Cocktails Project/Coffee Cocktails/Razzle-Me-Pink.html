Razzle Me Pink is tasteful average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml raspberry liqueur massenee and 30ml coffee liqueur with 10ml grenadine giffard as well as 30ml milk including 50ml ice and served with sweet dishes. Just fill glass with ice to chill then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass over ice and garnish with wild raspberries and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
