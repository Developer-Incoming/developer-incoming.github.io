West Coast Paralyzer is delicious medium cocktail at 0.7 standard drinks. Built with 15ml tequila blanco gran orendain and 15ml coffee liqueur fair with 35ml milk as well as 35ml root beer soda including 190ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just fill glass with ice then add half a nip of tequila to glass and pour in root beer soda. Float milk and sprinkle coffee liqueur on top and served in a highball glass cold.
