Jamaica Joe is tasteful very strong cocktail at 2.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum dark and 30ml coffee liqueur caf del mar with 30ml advocaat as well as 10ml grenadine including 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
