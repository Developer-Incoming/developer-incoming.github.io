Twin Bean Frappe is luscious quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 45ml coffee liqueur koval and 15ml vanilla schnapps with 60ml ice and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just fill glass with shaved ice then pour ingredients over ice and garnish with two coffee beans and served in a saucer glass cold.
