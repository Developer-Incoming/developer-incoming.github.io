Ingredients
30 ml
Bourbon Makers Mark
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Borghetti
15 ml
Sugar Syrup
30 ml
Cream
20 ml
Soda Water
100 ml
Ice
14% Alcohol By Volume
28 Proof
1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker, less soda.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Add soda water.
  6. Tasteful
Served
Stirred
Cold
Martini Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Bourbon Lift is tasteful full bodied cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml bourbon makers mark and 15ml coffee liqueur borghetti with 15ml sugar syrup as well as 30ml cream including 20ml soda water also 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker, less soda and shake well. Strain into glass and add soda water and served in a martini glass cold.
