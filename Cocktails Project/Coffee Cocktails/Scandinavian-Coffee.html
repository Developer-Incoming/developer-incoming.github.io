Ingredients
60 ml
Aquavit Lehment
190 ml
Coffee Brew
Nutmeg
10% Alcohol By Volume
20 Proof
2 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add aquavit to glass.
  4. Pour in warm coffee.
  5. Add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste.
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Garnish with nutmeg.
  8. Palatable
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Scandinavian Coffee is palatable standard cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Built with 60ml aquavit lehment and 190ml coffee brew and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and add aquavit to glass. Pour in warm coffee and add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and garnish with nutmeg and served in a coffee glass warm.
