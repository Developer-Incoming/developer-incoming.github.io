John Alden is scrumptious very strong cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml curacao orange and 30ml rum gold amber with 30ml coffee liqueur walders as well as 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just place ice into mixing glass then pour all ingredients into mixing glass and stir well until mixed. Strain into glass and served in a martini glass cold.
