Ingredients
45 ml
Coffee Liqueur Decaf Richardos
15 ml
Tequila Reposado Mala Vida
30 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice
7% Alcohol By Volume
14 Proof
0.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place a small amount of ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Vigorously shake.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Top glass with ice.
  6. Restoring
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl
Also See: Dirty Mother

El Borracho is restoring mild cocktail at 0.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml coffee liqueur decaf richardos and 15ml tequila reposado mala vida with 30ml cream as well as 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just place a small amount of ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
