Ingredients
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur
15 ml
Whisky Cream
15 ml
Cinnamon Liqueur Bols
15 ml
Cream
30 ml
Coffee Brew
100 ml
Ice
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Brew coffee and chill.
  2. Add half the ice to shaker.
  3. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  4. Quick shake.
  5. Pour everything into glass.
  6. Add more ice to glass.
  7. Mouth Watering
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Midnight Manx is mouth watering average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml coffee liqueur and 15ml whisky cream with 15ml cinnamon liqueur bols as well as 15ml cream including 30ml coffee brew also 100ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just brew coffee and chill then add half the ice to shaker and add ingredients into shaker over ice. Quick shake and pour everything into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
