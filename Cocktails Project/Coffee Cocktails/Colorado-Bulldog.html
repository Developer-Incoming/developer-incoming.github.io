Colorado Bulldog is gorgeous bev dog 357 strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml vodka and 30ml coffee liqueur hellfire with 50ml cream as well as 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and garnish with toasted hazelnuts and served in a saucer glass cold.
