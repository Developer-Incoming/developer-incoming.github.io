Old San Francisco is very palatable strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml cherry liqueur kirsch bols and 30ml coffee liqueur mr black with 10ml lime syrup as well as 10ml grenadine including 20ml cream also 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
