Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.

120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Tira Misu is finger licking strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml brandy and 15ml coffee liqueur with 15ml chocolate liqueur brown as well as 30ml cream including 10ml egg yolk also 10ml cheese mascarpone together with 100ml ice and served with sweet dishes. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake the hell out of it. Strain into glass and flick a little chocolate powder on top and served in a saucer glass cold.
