Method - Shaken - Cold

  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Quick shake to mix.

120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Glaucoma is rejuvenating quite strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml vodka and 15ml gin with 15ml rum spiced malibu as well as 15ml coffee liqueur cantera negra including 30ml lemon juice also 10ml sugar syrup together with 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just place ice into shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and quick shake to mix. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a saucer glass cold.
