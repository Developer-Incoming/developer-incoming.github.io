30 ml

30 ml

100 ml

1 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Add ice to mixing glass.

  4. Strain into glass over a little ice.
  5. Bewitching
Served

Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Jaw Breaker is bewitching full bodied cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee liqueur vedrenne and 30ml banana liqueur with 30ml sambuca ramazzotti as well as 100ml ice and best at tea time. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass over a little ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
