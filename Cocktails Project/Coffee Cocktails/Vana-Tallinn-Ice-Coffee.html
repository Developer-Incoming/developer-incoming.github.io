Vana Tallinn Ice Coffee is freshening above average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml vana tallinn and 30ml coffee brew with 30ml milk as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
