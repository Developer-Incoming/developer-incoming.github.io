Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add brandy to glass.
  4. Pour in sugar.
  5. Stir in coffee.
  6. Add whipped cream
  7. Best serving temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  8. Sprinkle shaved chocolate on top.

Rudesheim Coffee is geschmackvollen smooth cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml brandy asbach and 110ml coffee brew with 10ml sugar syrup brown and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and add brandy to glass. Pour in sugar and stir in coffee and add whipped cream and best serving temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and sprinkle shaved chocolate on top and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
