Whistling Gypsy is delectable very strong cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml coffee liqueur seymours and 30ml whisky cream with 30ml vodka vanilla margaret river and best with dessert type dishes. Just pour coffee liqueur into bottom of glass then float whisky cream and add a layer of vanilla vodka on top and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
