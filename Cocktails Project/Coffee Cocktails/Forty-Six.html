Ingredients
30 ml
Rum Gold Amber
15 ml
Banana Liqueur
15 ml
Chocolate Liqueur
30 ml
Coffee Brew
100 ml
Ice
Banana
21% Alcohol By Volume
42 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Brew and chill coffee.
  2. Place ice into shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Garnish with banana slice.
  7. Stimulating
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Forty Six is stimulating quite strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum gold amber and 15ml banana liqueur with 15ml chocolate liqueur as well as 30ml coffee brew including 100ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just brew and chill coffee then place ice into shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass and garnish with banana slice and served in a saucer glass cold.
