Ingredients
45 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur Giffard
30 ml
Chocolate Syrup
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Copa De Oro
20 ml
Coffee Brew
130 ml
Ice
13% Alcohol By Volume
26 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Brew coffee and chill.
  2. Add ice to blender.
  3. Add all ingredients to blender.
  4. Blend ruff.
  5. Pour into glass.
  6. Delish
Served
Blended
Cold

250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Net Crusher is delish above average cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Blended with 45ml hazelnut liqueur giffard and 30ml chocolate syrup with 15ml coffee liqueur copa de oro as well as 20ml coffee brew including 130ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just brew coffee and chill then add ice to blender and add all ingredients to blender. Blend ruff and pour into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
