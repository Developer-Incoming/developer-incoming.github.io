Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Brew coffee.
  2. Warm glass.
  3. Warm mixing glass and heat cubes.
  4. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  5. Stir ingredients together until mixed.
  6. Strain into glass.
  7. Garnish with a few twist of peel.

Cafe Diable is flavoursome very strong cocktail at 2.1 standard drinks. Built with 60ml rakija bovin fine and 15ml coffee brew with 10ml clove syrup as well as 15ml orange juice and best at tea time. Just brew coffee then warm glass and warm mixing glass and heat cubes. Add ingredients to mixing glass and stir ingredients together until mixed and strain into glass and garnish with a few twist of peel and served in a martini glass warm.
