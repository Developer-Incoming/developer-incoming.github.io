Fireside Chat is flavorous heavy cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Stirred with 45ml bourbon high west and 15ml coffee liqueur with 10ml bitters walnut black as well as 10ml vanilla syrup including 100ml ice and best with vegetable and vegetarian dishes. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and garnish with a dusting of nutmeg and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
