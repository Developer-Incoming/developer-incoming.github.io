15 ml

10 ml

100 ml

2 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Pack ice onto glass.
  2. Splash in orange bitters.
  3. Pour in bourbon.
  4. Add coffee liqueur on top.
  5. Garnish with orange twist.
  6. Nice
Served
Poured

200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Revolver is nice full strength cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Built with 45ml bourbon evan williams and 15ml coffee liqueur with 10ml bitters orange as well as 100ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just pack ice onto glass then splash in orange bitters and pour in bourbon. Add coffee liqueur on top and garnish with orange twist and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
