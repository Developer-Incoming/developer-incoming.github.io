Street Shooter is restoring very strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Stirred with 10ml almond liqueur and 10ml coffee liqueur cafe rica with 10ml hazelnut liqueur as well as 10ml whisky cream including 20ml vodka 666 autumn butter and best with lunch. Just add ingredients to mixing glass then stir well and pour ingredients into glass and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
