Ingredients
20 ml
Whisky Cream
20 ml
Coffee Liqueur
20 ml
Peppermint Liqueur
20 ml
Banana Liqueur
21% Alcohol By Volume
42 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Room Temperature
  1. Place coffee liqueur into glass.
  2. Layer in coffee liqueur.
  3. Float mint liqueur on top.
  4. Add layer of banana liqueur on top.
  5. Bracing
Served
Layered
Room Temperature
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Horny Monkey is bracing quite strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml whisky cream and 20ml coffee liqueur with 20ml peppermint liqueur as well as 20ml banana liqueur and best with buffet style food. Just place coffee liqueur into glass then layer in coffee liqueur and float mint liqueur on top. Add layer of banana liqueur on top and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
