War Cloud is palatable very strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml vodka 666 autumn butter and 15ml coffee liqueur 7k distillery with 15ml brandy as well as 15ml whisky cream including 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a sherry glass cold.
