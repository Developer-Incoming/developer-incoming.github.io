Method - Poured - Warm

Pumpkin Spiced Latte is very enjoyable standard cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Built with 30ml pumpkin liqueur mozart and 30ml coffee liqueur jagermeister with 90ml milk and good to have with brunch. Just warm glass then pour in pumpkin liqueur and add coffee liqueur to glass. Stir in hot milk and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
