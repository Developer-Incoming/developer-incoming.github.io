Method - Shaken - Cold

Riviera is flavoursome strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 20ml brandy vieux dekuyper and 20ml creme de cacao brown with 10ml vermouth bitter punt e mes as well as 10ml coffee liqueur including 30ml cream also 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker, less cream and shake well. Strain into glass and float cream on top and served in a sherry glass cold.
