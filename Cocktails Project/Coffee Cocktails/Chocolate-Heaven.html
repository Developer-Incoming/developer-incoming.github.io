Ingredients
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Gabriel Boudier
30 ml
Chocolate Liqueur White Mozart
15 ml
Creme de Cacao Brown
50 ml
Cream
10 ml
Chocolate Shavings
120 ml
Ice
10% Alcohol By Volume
20 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Add ice to blender.
  2. Add all ingredients.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Garnish with chocolate shavings.
  5. Luscious
Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl
Also See: Chocolate Hell

Chocolate Heaven is luscious standard cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Blended with 15ml coffee liqueur gabriel boudier and 30ml chocolate liqueur white mozart with 15ml creme de cacao brown as well as 50ml cream including 10ml chocolate shavings also 120ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients and blend until smooth and garnish with chocolate shavings and served in a poco grande glass cold.
