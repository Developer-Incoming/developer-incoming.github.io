Kamora Takes Manhattan is appealing very strong cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee liqueur kamora and 30ml bourbon knob creek with 20ml vermouth sweet as well as 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and shake well. Stir well and garnish with maraschino and coffee bean and served in a martini glass cold.
