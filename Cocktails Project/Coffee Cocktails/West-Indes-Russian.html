West Indes Russian is dainty full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Built with 15ml vodka russian standard and 15ml rum reserva angostura with 15ml coffee liqueur tia maria as well as 55ml cola soda including 190ml ice and best with lunch. Just fill highball glass with ice cubes then add half a shot of vodka to glass and pour in half a shot of rum. Add cola soda to glass and drizzle coffee liqueur on top and served in a highball glass cold.
