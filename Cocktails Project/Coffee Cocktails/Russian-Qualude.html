Russian Qualude is scrumptious strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml vodka white birch and 15ml orange liqueur with 15ml hazelnut liqueur as well as 15ml coffee liqueur including 15ml whisky cream also 25ml cream together with 100ml ice and best at tea time. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Hawthorne strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
