Drambuie Espresso Martini is stimulating robust cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml whisky liqueur drambuie and 30ml whisky monkey shoulder with 30ml coffee brew as well as 10ml vanilla syrup including 100ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just put ice cubes into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake well. Hawthorne strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
