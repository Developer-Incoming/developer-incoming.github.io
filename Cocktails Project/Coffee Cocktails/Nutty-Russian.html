Ingredients
30 ml
Vodka Stolichnaya Elit
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur
30 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur
100 ml
Ice
Walnut
29% Alcohol By Volume
58 Proof
2.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Add vodka to glass.
  3. Add coffee liqueur.
  4. Pour in hazelnut liqueur.
  5. Garnish with walnut.
  6. Delish
Served
Poured
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Nutty Russian is delish very strong cocktail at 2.1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml vodka stolichnaya elit and 30ml coffee liqueur with 30ml hazelnut liqueur as well as 100ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just pack ice into glass then add vodka to glass and add coffee liqueur. Pour in hazelnut liqueur and garnish with walnut and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
