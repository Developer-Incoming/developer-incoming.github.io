Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm old fashioned glass.
  2. Add coffee liqueur to glass.
  3. Bew espresso coffee and add to glass.
  4. Steam milk with foam.
  5. Add milk and foam to glass.
  6. Serve with a warm croissant pastry.
  7. Scrumptious

Cappuccino is scrumptious diluted cocktail at 0.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur vok and 30ml coffee brew with 120ml milk hot and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm old fashioned glass then add coffee liqueur to glass and bew espresso coffee and add to glass. Steam milk with foam and add milk and foam to glass and serve with a warm croissant pastry and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
