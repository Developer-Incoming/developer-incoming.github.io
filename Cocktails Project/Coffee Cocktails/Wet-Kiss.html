Wet Kiss is delicious full bodied cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Blended with 20ml sambuca bottega and 20ml brandy coffee allens with 20ml whisky cream as well as 60ml cream including 120ml ice and best at tea time. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
