60 ml
Coffee Liqueur Tia Maria Dark
Chocolate Syrup
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Room Temperature

  2. Warm two glass.

  5. Succulent

Skid Marks is succulent quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 60ml coffee liqueur tia maria dark and best when a coffee is needed. Just two shots always share a shot then warm two glass and pour coffee liqueur into shot glass. Add a dribble of chocolate syrup and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
