Judgement Day Shot is tasty mature cocktail at 2.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 10ml coffee liqueur and 10ml jagermeister with 10ml mint liqueur as well as 15ml rum 151 proof including 15ml everclear 190 proof and best with lunch. Just two shots always share a shot then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour into two shot glasses and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
