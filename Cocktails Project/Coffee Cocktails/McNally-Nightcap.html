McNally Nightcap is mouth watering nightcap full bodied cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Built with 100ml coffee brew and 60ml whisky balvenie 14year and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just warm balloon and coffee glass then add whisky to balloon and add brewed coffee to glass and add side serve of shortbread and crackers and served in a balloon glass at room temperature.
