Method - Layered - Warm

  2. Brew coffee.

Galliano Hotshot is fortifying standard cocktail at 0.5 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml galliano and 20ml coffee brew with 20ml cream and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass, then brew coffee and warm cream. Add galliano to warm glass and float warm coffee and foat warm cream on top and served in a sherry glass warm.
