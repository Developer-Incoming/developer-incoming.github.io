Quick Decision is beguiling strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml bourbon bowman brothers and 10ml whisky cream with 10ml rum black as well as 10ml coffee liqueur including 50ml cream also 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
