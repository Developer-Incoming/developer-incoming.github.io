Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur ColdPresso
30 ml
Vodka Coffee Hunter
60 ml
Coffee Brew
130 ml
Milk Hot
7% Alcohol By Volume
14 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Heat coffee glass.
  2. Brew coffee and steam milk.
  3. Add coffee liqueur to glass.
  4. Pour in vodka.
  5. Add two shots of brewed coffee.
  6. Top with warm steamed milk.
  7. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  8. Very Palatable

Caffe latte or Caffe e latte or caffelatte or caffellatte is a coffee drink made with coffee and steamed milk

Served
Poured
Warm

Double Latte is very palatable mild cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur coldpresso and 30ml vodka coffee hunter with 60ml coffee brew as well as 130ml milk hot and best when a coffee is needed. Just heat coffee glass then brew coffee and steam milk and add coffee liqueur to glass. Pour in vodka and add two shots of brewed coffee and top with warm steamed milk and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
