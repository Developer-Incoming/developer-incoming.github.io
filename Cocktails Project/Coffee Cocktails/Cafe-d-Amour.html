Ingredients
220 ml
Coffee Brew
30 ml
Cognac Hennessy XO
Lemon Peel
Cinnamon
Pastry Danish Apricot
5% Alcohol By Volume
10 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add all ingredients to warm glass.
  4. Pour in warm coffee.
  5. Add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste.
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Serve with apricot danish pastry.
  8. Garnish with peel and cinnamon.
  9. Very Palatable
Served
Poured

Cafe d Amour is very palatable light cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 220ml coffee brew and 30ml cognac hennessy xo and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and add all ingredients to warm glass. Pour in warm coffee and add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste then optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and serve with apricot danish pastry and garnish with peel and cinnamon and served in a coffee glass warm.
