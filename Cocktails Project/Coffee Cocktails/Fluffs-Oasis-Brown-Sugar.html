15 ml

Ice

Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Fill glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add whisk cream to glass.
  3. Pour in coffee liqueur.
  4. Top with root beer soda.

Poured

Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Fluffs Oasis Brown Sugar is medium cocktail at 0.7 standard drinks. Built with 30ml whisky cream and 15ml coffee liqueur giffard with 55ml root beer soda as well as 190ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just fill glass with ice cubes then add whisk cream to glass and pour in coffee liqueur. Top with root beer soda and served in a highball glass cold.
