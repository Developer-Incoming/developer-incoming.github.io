15 ml

Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Fill glass with ice.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.

Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Castaway is dainty quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml rum dark and 15ml coffee liqueur bottega with 40ml pineapple juice as well as 190ml ice and best at tea time. Just fill glass with ice then add all ingredients to shaker and quick dry shake without ice. Pour into glass over ice and garnish with pineapple and served in a highball glass cold.
