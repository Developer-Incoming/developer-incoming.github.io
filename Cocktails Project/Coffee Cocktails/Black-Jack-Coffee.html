Black Jack Coffee is scrumptious quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml brandy kwv 20year and 15ml cherry liqueur soplica with 30ml coffee brew as well as 10ml sugar syrup brown including 100ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
