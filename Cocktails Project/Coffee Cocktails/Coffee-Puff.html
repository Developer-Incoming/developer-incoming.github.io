Ingredients
60 ml
Coffee Liqueur Wild Wombat
70 ml
Milk
70 ml
Soda Water
Coffee Bean
6% Alcohol By Volume
12 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker, less soda.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Add soda.
  6. Garnish with coffee beans.
  7. Flavorous
Served
Shaken
Cold
Goblet Glass
360ml, 12oz, 36cl

Coffee Puff is flavorous weak cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 60ml coffee liqueur wild wombat and 70ml milk with 70ml soda water as well as 100ml and served with or for breakfast. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker, less soda and shake well. Strain into glass and add soda and garnish with coffee beans and served in a goblet glass cold.
