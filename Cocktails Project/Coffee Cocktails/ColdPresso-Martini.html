Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur NV Rubbens
30 ml
Vodka MRDC
20 ml
Coffee Brew
10 ml
Sugar Syrup
10 ml
Egg White
100 ml
Ice
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Brew and chill coffee.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Very Enjoyable
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass

ColdPresso Martini is very enjoyable quite strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur nv rubbens and 30ml vodka mrdc with 20ml coffee brew as well as 10ml sugar syrup including 10ml egg white also 100ml ice and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just brew and chill coffee then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
