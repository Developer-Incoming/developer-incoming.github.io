20 ml

20 ml

Cream
20 ml

130 ml
Ice

Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Add ice to blender.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend until grainy.
  4. Pour into glass.
  5. Drizzle a little honey on top.
  6. Beguiling

Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Toblerone Frappe is beguiling average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Blended with 20ml hazelnut liqueur and 20ml coffee liqueur with 20ml whisky cream as well as 30ml cream including 20ml honey also 130ml ice and served with or for breakfast. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until grainy. Pour into glass and drizzle a little honey on top and served in a poco grande glass cold.
