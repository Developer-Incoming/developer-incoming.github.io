Ingredients
45 ml
Whisky Cream
15 ml
Whiskey Irish
40 ml
Coffee Brew
190 ml
Ice
14% Alcohol By Volume
28 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Make and chill coffee.
  2. Pack ice into glass.
  3. Add ice to shaker.
  4. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  5. Shake well.
  6. Strain into glass over ice.
  7. Moreish
Served
Shaken
Cold
Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Leprechauns Coffee is moreish full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml whisky cream and 15ml whiskey irish with 40ml coffee brew as well as 190ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just make and chill coffee then pack ice into glass and add ice to shaker. Add all ingredients to shaker and shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
