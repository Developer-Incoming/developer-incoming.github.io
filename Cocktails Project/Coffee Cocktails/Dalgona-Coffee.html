Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Scylla
30 ml
Rum Dark Isautier
100 ml
Coffee Brew
90 ml
Soy Milk
30 ml
Cream Whipped
10 ml
Honeycomb Dalgona
6% Alcohol By Volume
12 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Brew coffee.
  2. Warm glass and milk.
  3. Add liqueur and rum to glass.
  4. Pour in long black coffee.
  5. Add in warm milk.
  6. Top with wipped cream.
  7. Crumble dalgona candy on top.
  8. Delightful
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Dalgona Coffee is delightful weak cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur scylla and 30ml rum dark isautier with 100ml coffee brew as well as 90ml soy milk also 30ml cream whipped together with 10ml honeycomb dalgona and best when a coffee is needed. Just brew coffee then warm glass and milk and add liqueur and rum to glass. Pour in long black coffee and add in warm milk and top with wipped cream and crumble dalgona candy on top and served in a coffee glass warm.
