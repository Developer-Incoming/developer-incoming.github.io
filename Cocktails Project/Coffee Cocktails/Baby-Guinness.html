Ingredients
50 ml
Coffee Liqueur Onyx
10 ml
Whisky Cream
Pastry Turnover
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Cold
  1. Add coffee liqueur to glass.
  2. Float whisky cream on top.
  3. Best taken with a turnover.
  4. Restoring
Served
Layered
Cold
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Baby Guinness is restoring quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 50ml coffee liqueur onyx and 10ml whisky cream and best with lunch. Just add coffee liqueur to glass then float whisky cream on top and best taken with a turnover and served in a sherry glass cold.
