Ingredients
30 ml
Whisky Scotch Tamdhu 15year
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Koval
30 ml
Whisky Cream
100 ml
Ice
28% Alcohol By Volume
56 Proof
2 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Pour ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Add more ice to glass.
  6. Delectable
Served
Poured
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Rugburn is delectable very strong cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Built with 30ml whisky scotch tamdhu 15year and 30ml coffee liqueur koval with 30ml whisky cream as well as 100ml ice and best at tea time. Just add half the ice to shaker then pour ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Pour everything into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
