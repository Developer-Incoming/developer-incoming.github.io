Ingredients
30 ml
Whisky Cream
10 ml
Coffee Syrup
30 ml
Egg Yolk
30 ml
Milk Hot
Heat Cubes
Nutmeg
5% Alcohol By Volume
10 Proof
0.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Warm milk
  3. Warm shaker and heat cubes.
  4. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  5. Emulsify by shake well.
  6. Pour into warm glass.
  7. Garnish with a sprinkle of nutmeg on top.
  8. Dainty Christmas Cocktail
Served
Shaken
Warm
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl
Also See: Christmas Flip

Egg Flip is dainty christmas cocktail light cocktail at 0.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml whisky cream and 10ml coffee syrup with 30ml egg yolk as well as 30ml milk hot and served for or with tiffins. Just warm glass then warm milk and warm shaker and heat cubes. Add all ingredients to shaker and emulsify by shake well and pour into warm glass and garnish with a sprinkle of nutmeg on top and served in a saucer glass warm.
