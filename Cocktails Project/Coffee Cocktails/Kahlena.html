Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur NV Rubbens
30 ml
Banana Liqueur Hiram Walker
60 ml
Cream
120 ml
Ice
Pineapple
11% Alcohol By Volume
22 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Add ice to blender.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into glass.
  5. Garnish with pineapple wedge.
  6. Mouth Watering
Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Kahlena is mouth watering average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml coffee liqueur nv rubbens and 30ml banana liqueur hiram walker with 60ml cream as well as 120ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and garnish with pineapple wedge and served in a poco grande glass cold.
