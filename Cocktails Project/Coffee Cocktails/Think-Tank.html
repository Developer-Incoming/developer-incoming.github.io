Think Tank is very enjoyable full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee liqueur sierra and 15ml vodka diamond doll silver with 30ml cream as well as 25ml soda water including 190ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just add half the ice to mixing glass then pour ingredients to mixing glass and stir well until mixed. Pour all into glass and top with ice and served in a highball glass cold.
