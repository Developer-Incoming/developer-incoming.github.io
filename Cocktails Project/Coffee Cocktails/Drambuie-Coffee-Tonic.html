Ingredients
30 ml
Whisky Liqueur Drambuie
30 ml
Tonic Water
40 ml
Coffee Brew
190 ml
Ice
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Make coffee and chill.
  2. Add ice to mixing glass.
  3. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Pour all into glass.
  6. Add more ice to glass.
  7. Restoring
Served
Stirred
Cold
Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Drambuie Coffee Tonic is restoring average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml whisky liqueur drambuie and 30ml tonic water with 40ml coffee brew as well as 190ml ice and good to have with brunch. Just make coffee and chill then add ice to mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Mix well and pour all into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a highball glass cold.
