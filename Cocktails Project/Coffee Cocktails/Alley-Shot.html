Alley Shot is bracing alley very strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Stirred with 10ml almond liqueur and 10ml coffee liqueur with 10ml hazelnut liqueur teichenne as well as 10ml whiskey cream coole swan including 20ml vodka and best with an entree or small snack. Just two shots always share a shot then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Pour ingredients into glass and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
