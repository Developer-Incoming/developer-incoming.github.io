Ingredients
30 ml
Butterscotch Liqueur Teichenne
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Koval
15 ml
Bourbon McKenzie
10 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice
Chocolate Syrup Torani
Butterscotch Syrup Monin
21% Alcohol By Volume
42 Proof
1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Ribbon chocolate syryp inside of glass.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker, omit butterscotch syrup.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Garnish with butterscotch syrup.
  7. Very Palatable
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Dark Star is very palatable quite strong cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml butterscotch liqueur teichenne and 15ml coffee liqueur koval with 15ml bourbon mckenzie as well as 10ml cream including 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just ribbon chocolate syryp inside of glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker, omit butterscotch syrup. Shake well and strain into glass and garnish with butterscotch syrup and served in a saucer glass cold.
