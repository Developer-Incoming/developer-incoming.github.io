Cinnamon Twister Shot is delicioso quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml cinnamon liqueur kahlua and 15ml whisky cream with 15ml coffee liqueur as well as 15ml butterscotch liqueur and best when a coffee is needed. Just two shots always share a shot then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake. Pour all into glass and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
