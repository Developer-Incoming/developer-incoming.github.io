Ingredients
30 ml
Rum Cream El Dorado
30 ml
Rum Spiced 100 Souls White
15 ml
Chocolate Liqueur White Mozart
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Cream Somrus
100 ml
Ice
24% Alcohol By Volume
48 Proof
1.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place some ice cubes into cocktail shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Quick shake to mix.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Top glass with ice.
  6. Agradavel
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Everglades Special is agradavel robust cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum cream el dorado and 30ml rum spiced 100 souls white with 15ml chocolate liqueur white mozart as well as 15ml coffee liqueur cream somrus including 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just place some ice cubes into cocktail shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and quick shake to mix. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
