Ingredients
60 ml
Strega
190 ml
Coffee Brew
Nutmeg
10% Alcohol By Volume
20 Proof
2 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add all ingredients to warm glass.
  4. Pour in warm coffee.
  5. Add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste.
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Garnish with nutmeg.
  8. Enjoyable
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl
