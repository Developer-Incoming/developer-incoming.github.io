Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur After Supreme
10 ml
Coffee Liqueur Cream Maestro Cafe
50 ml
Ice Cream Coffee
20 ml
Cream Whipped
Cocktail Spoon
8% Alcohol By Volume
16 Proof
0.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Chill saucer gass.
  2. Add a little whipped cream to bottom of glass.
  3. Pour in nip of coffee liqueur around edges.
  4. Add scoop of coffee ice cream to center of glass.
  5. Top with whipped cream.
  6. Drizzle coffee cream on top.
  7. Serve with a spoon.
  8. Delectable
Served
Poured
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Cafe Liegeois is delectable smooth cocktail at 0.7 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur after supreme and 10ml coffee liqueur cream maestro cafe with 50ml ice cream coffee as well as 20ml cream whipped and best with dessert type dishes. Just chill saucer gass then add a little whipped cream to bottom of glass and pour in nip of coffee liqueur around edges. Add scoop of coffee ice cream to center of glass and top with whipped cream and drizzle coffee cream on top and serve with a spoon and served in a saucer glass cold.
