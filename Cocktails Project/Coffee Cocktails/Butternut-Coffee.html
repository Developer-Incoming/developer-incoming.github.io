Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Brew coffee.
  2. Warm glass.
  3. Add butterscotch liqueur to glass.
  4. Pour in amaretto liqueur.
  5. Add coffee.
  6. Add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste.
  7. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  8. Delectable

Butternut Coffee is delectable mild cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml butterscotch liqueur ole smoky and 30ml amaretto liqueur ole smoky with 190ml coffee brew and served for or with tiffins. Just brew coffee then warm glass and add butterscotch liqueur to glass. Pour in amaretto liqueur and add coffee and add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
