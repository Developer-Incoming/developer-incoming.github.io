15 ml

Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Chill glass until cold.
  2. Add scoop of ice cream to glass.
  3. Pour over whisky cream.
  4. Sprinkle ovaltine powder on top.
  5. Moreish

Ovaltine Bombe is moreish standard cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 45ml coffee liqueur cafe rica and 50ml ice cream with 15ml ovaltine powder and best with dessert type dishes. Just chill glass until cold then add scoop of ice cream to glass and pour over whisky cream. Sprinkle ovaltine powder on top and served in a saucer glass cold.
