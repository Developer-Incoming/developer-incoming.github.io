15 ml

50 ml
Milk
100 ml
Ice
8% Alcohol By Volume
16 Proof

Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Vigorously shake.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Freshening
Served
Shaken

Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

After Eighteen is freshening smooth cocktail at 0.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml coffee liqueur giffard and 15ml peppermint liqueur white bols with 10ml chocolate syrup as well as 50ml milk including 100ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
