20 ml

200 ml

26% Alcohol By Volume

Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Delicious
Served

Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Shes Paying is delicious very strong cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 20ml vodka thunder toffee and 20ml coffee liqueur with 10ml orange liqueur as well as 10ml almond liqueur including 200ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
