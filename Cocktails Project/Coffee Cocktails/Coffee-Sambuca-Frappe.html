60 ml

Coffee Sambuca Frappe is scrumptious very strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee liqueur hellfire and 30ml sambuca white galliano with 60ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just fill glass with shaved ice then add ingredients to mixing glass and mix well. Pour into glass over ice and served in a saucer glass cold.
