Teddy Bear is scrummy average cocktail at 0.6 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml coffee liqueur and 15ml peppermint liqueur with 15ml cream as well as 15ml cola soda including 100ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just place ice into mixing glass then add all ingredients to mixing glass and stir ingredients together until mixed. Pour all into glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
