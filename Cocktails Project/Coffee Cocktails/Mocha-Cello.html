Ingredients

30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Dolce
30 ml
Coffee Brew
100 ml
Ice
Chocolate Shavings
11% Alcohol By Volume
22 Proof
0.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Garnish with large chocolate shaving.
  7. Scrumptious
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Mocha Cello is scrumptious average cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml chocolate liqueur dolce and 30ml coffee liqueur dolce with 30ml coffee brew as well as 100ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and garnish with large chocolate shaving and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
