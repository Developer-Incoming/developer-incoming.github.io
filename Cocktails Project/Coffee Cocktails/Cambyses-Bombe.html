Method - Poured - Cold

  2. Pour coconut cream over ice cream.
  3. In one go pour rum and coffee liqueur into glass along each side.
  4. Serve with cocktail spoon.
  5. Garnish with coconut flesh.

Cambyses Bombe is flavoursome full bodied cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Built with 50ml ice cream coconut and 30ml rum light white with 30ml coffee liqueur manly spirits as well as 10ml coconut cream and best with lunch. Just add scoop of coconut ice cream to glass then pour coconut cream over ice cream and in one go pour rum and coffee liqueur into glass along each side and serve with cocktail spoon and garnish with coconut flesh and served in a saucer glass cold.
