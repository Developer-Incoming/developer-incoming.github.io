Coffee Liqueur
15 ml

55 ml
Milk
190 ml
Ice
13% Alcohol By Volume
26 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Pour ingredients into bar shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Exquisite

Cold

Kialoa is exquisite above average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur and 15ml rum with 55ml milk as well as 190ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just add half the ice to shaker then pour ingredients into bar shaker over ice and shake well. Pour everything into glass and fill glass with ice and served in a highball glass cold.
