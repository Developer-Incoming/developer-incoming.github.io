Ingredients
45 ml
Brandy St Not Your Nannas
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Bols
10 ml
Egg White
200 ml
Ice
28% Alcohol By Volume
56 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Fill glass with ice to chill.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Rest
  6. Shake again.
  7. Strain into glass over ice.
  8. Inspiriting

Torpedo: a cigar shaped self propelled underwater missile

Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Torpedo is inspiriting very strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml brandy st not your nannas and 15ml coffee liqueur bols with 10ml egg white as well as 200ml ice and best with lunch. Just fill glass with ice to chill then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and rest and shake again then strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
