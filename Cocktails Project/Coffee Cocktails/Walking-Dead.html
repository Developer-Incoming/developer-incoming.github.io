Walking Dead is appealing full bodied cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml vodka moscato grape skyy and 30ml coffee liqueur flaschengeist with 60ml cream as well as 10ml caramel syrup monin including 10ml chocolate syrup also 10ml grenadine together with 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients, less last three and shake well. Run syrup and grenadine around inside glass and strain into glass and garnish with red dust and served in a saucer glass cold.
