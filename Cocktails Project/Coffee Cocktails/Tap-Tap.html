Tap Tap is bracing strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Layered with 45ml whiskey ole smoky coffee and 45ml cranberry juice white and best with buffet style food. Just warm thick base old fashioned glass then add whisky to glass and pour in craberry juice and tap twice and drink in one go and served in a old fashioned glass at room temperature.
