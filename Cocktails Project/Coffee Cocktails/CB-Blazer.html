Brandy Christian Brothers Sacred Bond
15 ml

205 ml
Coffee Brew
6% Alcohol By Volume
12 Proof
1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm coffee style glass.
  2. Brew long black coffee.
  3. Add brandy into glass.
  4. Pour in coffee.
  5. Stir in honey.
  6. Best serving temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste.
  8. Finger Licking
Served
Poured

CB Blazer is finger licking weak cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Built with 30ml brandy christian brothers sacred bond and 15ml honey dark with 205ml coffee brew and best with morning tea snacks. Just warm coffee style glass then brew long black coffee and add brandy into glass. Pour in coffee and stir in honey and best serving temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste and served in a coffee glass warm.
