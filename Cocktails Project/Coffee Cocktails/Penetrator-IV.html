Penetrator IV is invigorating quite strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 10ml galliano and 10ml whisky cream with 10ml coffee liqueur as well as 10ml tequila gold including 10ml vodka also 10ml rum coconut together with 30ml milk and 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
