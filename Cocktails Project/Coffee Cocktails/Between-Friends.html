Between Friends is tasty standard cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Built with 30ml butterscotch liqueur ole smoky and 30ml coffee liqueur toussaint with 90ml milk as well as 90ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just place ice into glass then add shot of butterscotch liqueur into glass and pour in milk. Sprinkle coffee liqueur on top and add a musk flavour doughnut on the side and served in a poco grande glass cold.
