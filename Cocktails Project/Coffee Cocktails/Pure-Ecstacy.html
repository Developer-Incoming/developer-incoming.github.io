Pure Ecstacy is delectable strong cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml whisky cream and 30ml coffee liqueur two brothers with 30ml vodka as well as 30ml milk including 120ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just put ice into blender then pour all ingredients into cocktail blender and blend until very smooth and served in a poco grande glass cold.
