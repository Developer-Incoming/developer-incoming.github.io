Ingredients
60 ml
Brandy
10 ml
Bitters Fernet Branca
10 ml
Coffee Liqueur
10 ml
Bitters Orange
50 ml
Raisin
24% Alcohol By Volume
48 Proof
2.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Room Temperature
  1. Cut raisin into small bits.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour ingredients into glass.
  5. Garnish with a few raisin on top.
  6. Delightful
Served
Shaken
Room Temperature
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Dr Zeus is delightful robust cocktail at 2.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 60ml brandy and 10ml bitters fernet branca with 10ml coffee liqueur as well as 10ml bitters orange including 50ml raisin and best with an entree or small snack. Just cut raisin into small bits then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Pour ingredients into glass and garnish with a few raisin on top and served in a old fashioned glass at room temperature.
