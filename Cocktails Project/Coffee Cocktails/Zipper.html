Ingredients
40 ml
Rum Cream Liqueur
10 ml
Coffee Liqueur Hellfire
10 ml
Whisky Cream Irish Feeneys Luxurious
50 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice
Chocolate Shavings
11% Alcohol By Volume
22 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Garnish with chocolate shavings.
  6. Glorious
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Zipper is glorious average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 40ml rum cream liqueur and 10ml coffee liqueur hellfire with 10ml whisky cream irish feeneys luxurious as well as 50ml cream including 100ml ice and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and garnish with chocolate shavings and served in a saucer glass cold.
