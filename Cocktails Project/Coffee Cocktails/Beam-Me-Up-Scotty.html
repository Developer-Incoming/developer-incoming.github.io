Ingredients
20 ml
Coffee Liqueur Lawrenny
20 ml
Banana Liqueur McGuinness
20 ml
Whisky Cream Sortilege
19% Alcohol By Volume
38 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Cold
  1. Add coffee liqueur to bottom of glass.
  2. Float banana liqueur using tha back of a spoon.
  3. Float whisky cream on top.
  4. Delish Digestive
Served
Layered
Cold
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Beam Me Up Scotty is delish digestive strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur lawrenny and 20ml banana liqueur mcguinness with 20ml whisky cream sortilege and works well with savoury style foods. Just add coffee liqueur to bottom of glass then float banana liqueur using tha back of a spoon and float whisky cream on top and served in a sherry glass cold.
