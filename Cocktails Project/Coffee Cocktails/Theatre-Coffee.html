Theatre Coffee is scrumptious average cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml whisky cream and 30ml chocolate liqueur with 10ml hazelnut liqueur as well as 10ml chocolate liqueur brown including 70ml coffee brew and taken as nightcap before bedtime. Just warm glass then bew coffee and pour in whisky cream. Add chocolate liqueurs and pour in hazelnut liqueur and add coffe to glass then add some whipped cream on top and garnish with grated chocolate and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
