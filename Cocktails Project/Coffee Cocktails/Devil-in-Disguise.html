Devil in Disguise: Elvis Presley Song

Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Devil in Disguise is tasty standard cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml coffee liqueur vedrenne and 30ml rum strawberry cruzan with 20ml strawberry pulp as well as 20ml coconut cream including 20ml cream also 120ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and garnish with strawberry and served in a poco grande glass cold.
