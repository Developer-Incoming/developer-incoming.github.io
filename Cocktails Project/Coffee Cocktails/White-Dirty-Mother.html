White Dirty Mother is pleasant tasting full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur los arango and 15ml tequila blanco los arango with 55ml milk as well as 190ml ice and served with or for breakfast. Just fill glass with ice then add tequila to glass and add coffee liqueur. Top with milk and served in a highball glass cold.
