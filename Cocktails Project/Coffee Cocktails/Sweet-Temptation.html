Ingredients
30 ml
Whisky Cream Irish Baileys
30 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur Frangelico
30 ml
Whisky
100 ml
Ice
Chocolate Powder
Coffee Bean
27% Alcohol By Volume
54 Proof
1.9 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Pour ingredients into bar shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Add more ice to glass.
  6. Garnish with coffee beans and chocolate.
  7. Scrummy
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Sweet Temptation is scrummy very strong cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml whisky cream irish baileys and 30ml hazelnut liqueur frangelico with 30ml whisky as well as 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just add half the ice to shaker then pour ingredients into bar shaker and shake well. Pour all into glass and add more ice to glass and garnish with coffee beans and chocolate and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
