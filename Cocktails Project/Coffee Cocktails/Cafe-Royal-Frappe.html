30 ml
Eau de Vigne Merlet
30 ml
Coffee Brew
160 ml
Ice
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Brew and chill coffee.
  2. Pack glass with shaved ice.
  3. Add ice to mixing glass.
  4. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  5. Stir well.
  6. Strain into glass over ice.
  7. Rejuvenating
Served
Stirred
Cold

120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Cafe Royal Frappe is rejuvenating quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml eau de vigne merlet and 30ml coffee brew with 160ml ice and best with lunch. Just brew and chill coffee then pack glass with shaved ice and add ice to mixing glass. Add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well and strain into glass over ice and served in a martini glass cold.
