Rum Dark
15 ml

10 ml
Bitters
30 ml
Lime Juice
100 ml
Ice
26% Alcohol By Volume
52 Proof
2.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold

Jamaican: relating to Jamaica or its people

Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Jamaican is delish very strong cocktail at 2.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml rum dark and 15ml coffee liqueur tia maria with 10ml bitters as well as 30ml lime juice including 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass over ice and served in a saucer glass cold.
