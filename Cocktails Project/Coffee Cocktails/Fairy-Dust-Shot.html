Fairy Dust Shot is tasty heavy cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur kahlua and 20ml whisky cream irish baileys with 20ml absinthe liqueur green fairy and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just two shots always share a shot then pour in coffee liqueur and float whisky cream. Float absinthe on top and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
