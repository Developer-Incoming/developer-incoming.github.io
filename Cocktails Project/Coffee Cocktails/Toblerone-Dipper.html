Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well until creamy.
  4. Strain into glass.

Toblerone Dipper is woow above average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml whisky cream and 15ml hazelnut liqueur with 15ml coffee liqueur as well as 15ml creme de cacao including 30ml cream also 10ml honey together with 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well until creamy. Strain into glass and serve on a doily plate and garnish with honey ripple and sponge biscuits for dipping and served in a saucer glass cold.
