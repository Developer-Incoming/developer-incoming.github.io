Network Special is zamatos heavy cocktail at 2.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum light white and 15ml brandy coffee with 15ml rum 151 proof as well as 30ml cream including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and vigorously shake. Hawthorne strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
