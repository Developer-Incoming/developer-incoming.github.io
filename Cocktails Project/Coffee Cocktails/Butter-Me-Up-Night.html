Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur New Norfolk
30 ml
Butterscotch Liqueur
20 ml
Custard
20 ml
Cream
10 ml
Butter
Heat Cubes
Peanut Brittle
15% Alcohol By Volume
30 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Warm shaker and heat cubes.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well until emulsified.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Garnish with peanut brittle.
  7. Luscious
Served
Shaken
Warm
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Butter Me Up Night is luscious full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur new norfolk and 30ml butterscotch liqueur with 20ml custard as well as 20ml cream including 10ml butter and best with dessert type dishes. Just warm glass then warm shaker and heat cubes and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well until emulsified and strain into glass and garnish with peanut brittle and served in a saucer glass warm.
