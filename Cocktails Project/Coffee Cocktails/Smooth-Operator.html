Smooth Operator is restoring medium cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml hazelnut liqueur and 15ml coffee liqueur sutherland with 15ml whisky cream as well as 60ml banana pulp including 30ml cream also 90ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until silky smooth. Pour into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
