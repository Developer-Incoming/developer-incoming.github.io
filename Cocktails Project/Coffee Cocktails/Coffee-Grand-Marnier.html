Ingredients
30 ml
Orange Liqueur Grand Marnier
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Los Arango
30 ml
Orange Juice
100 ml
Ice
25% Alcohol By Volume
50 Proof
1.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake to mix.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Scrummy
Served
Shaken
Cold
Flute Glass
150ml, 5oz, 15cl

Coffee Grand Marnier is scrummy robust cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml orange liqueur grand marnier and 30ml coffee liqueur los arango with 30ml orange juice as well as 100ml ice and best with buffet style food. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Strain into glass and served in a flute glass cold.
