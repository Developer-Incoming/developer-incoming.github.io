Ingredients
30 ml
Rum Gold Amber
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Sabroso
30 ml
Pineapple Juice
10 ml
Lemon Juice
190 ml
Ice
19% Alcohol By Volume
38 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Energizing
Served
Shaken
Cold
Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Playboy Cooler is energizing strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum gold amber and 30ml coffee liqueur sabroso with 30ml pineapple juice as well as 10ml lemon juice including 190ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Pour everything into glass and fill glass with ice and served in a highball glass cold.
