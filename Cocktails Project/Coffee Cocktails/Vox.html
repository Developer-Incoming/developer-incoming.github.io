30 ml

10 ml

100 ml

1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Layer crepes with mascarpone cheese on each layer to make crepe cake.
  2. Alow to chill, then cut and add to glass.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over crepe cake.
  6. Sprinkle a little praline on top.
  7. Finger Licking

Vox is finger licking average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur scylla and 30ml butterscotch liqueur with 10ml cream as well as 50ml pancake crepe cake including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just layer crepes with mascarpone cheese on each layer to make crepe cake then alow to chill, then cut and add to glass and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over crepe cake and sprinkle a little praline on top and served in a saucer glass cold.
