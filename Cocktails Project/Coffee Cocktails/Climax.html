Ingredients
30 ml
Whiskey Southern Comfort
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur BVLand
30 ml
Cream
200 ml
Ice
16% Alcohol By Volume
32 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Fill glass with ice to chill.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Very Enjoyable
Served
Shaken
Cold
