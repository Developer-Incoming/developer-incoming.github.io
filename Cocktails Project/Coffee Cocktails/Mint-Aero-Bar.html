Ingredients
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Cream Finbar OLearys
15 ml
Creme de Menthe
15 ml
Whisky Cream
10 ml
Sugar Syrup Gelatin
45 ml
Milk
190 ml
Ice
Mint
8% Alcohol By Volume
16 Proof
0.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice cubes into glass.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Make frothy with a quick shake.
  4. Pour into glass over ice.
  5. Garnish with mint leaf.
  6. Delightful
Served
Shaken
Cold
