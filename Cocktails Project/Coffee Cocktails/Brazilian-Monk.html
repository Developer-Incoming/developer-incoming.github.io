Brazilian Monk is mouth watering strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Blended with 20ml hazelnut liqueur and 20ml coffee liqueur vittorio spirits with 20ml creme de cacao brown as well as 30ml sherry dry including 30ml ice cream vanilla also 120ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and garnish with grated chocolate and served in a poco grande glass cold.
