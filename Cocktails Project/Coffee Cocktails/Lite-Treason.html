Lite Treason is tasteful quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml mezcal scorpion and 15ml vodka with 15ml tequila coffee ponchos as well as 15ml amaro di angostura including 10ml sugar syrup also 30ml cream together with 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and top with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
