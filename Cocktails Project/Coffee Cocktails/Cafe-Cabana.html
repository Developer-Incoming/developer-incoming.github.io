Cafe Cabana is restoring mild cocktail at 0.6 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur and 70ml soda water with 190ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just pack ice into glass then add half the soda water to glass and pour in shot of coffee liqueur. Top glass with soda water and served in a highball glass cold.
