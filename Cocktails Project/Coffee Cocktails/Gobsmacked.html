Ingredients
30 ml
Rum Dark Plantation
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Tia Maria
30 ml
Milk
100 ml
Ice
Spice Mix Jamaican
21% Alcohol By Volume
42 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake to mix.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Sprinkle a little mixed Jamaican spice on top.
  7. Delectable

Gobsmacked: shocked and surprised beyond belief

Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Gobsmacked is delectable quite strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum dark plantation and 30ml coffee liqueur tia maria with 30ml milk as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and sprinkle a little mixed jamaican spice on top and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
