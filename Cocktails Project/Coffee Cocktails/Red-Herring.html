Ingredients
30 ml
Gin 23rd Street
15 ml
Brandy Cherry Vok
15 ml
Lemon Juice
10 ml
Sugar Syrup
10 ml
Coffee Brew
100 ml
Ice
19% Alcohol By Volume
38 Proof
1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Bew a little coffee.
  3. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  4. Gently dry stir without ice.
  5. Julep strain into glass.
  6. Very Enjoyable
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Red Herring is very enjoyable strong cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml gin 23rd street and 15ml brandy cherry vok with 15ml lemon juice as well as 10ml sugar syrup including 10ml coffee brew also 100ml ice and had with salad dishes. Just pack ice into glass then bew a little coffee and add ingredients to mixing glass. Gently dry stir without ice and julep strain into glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
