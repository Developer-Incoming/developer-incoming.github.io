Ingredients
30 ml
Raspberry Liqueur
30 ml
Whisky Cream
10 ml
Chocolate Liqueur
10 ml
Coffee Liqueur Flaschengeist
10 ml
Orange Liqueur
100 ml
Ice
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Hawthorne strain into glass.
  5. Dainty
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Raspberry Truffle is dainty quite strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml raspberry liqueur and 30ml whisky cream with 10ml chocolate liqueur as well as 10ml coffee liqueur flaschengeist including 10ml orange liqueur also 100ml ice and served before a meal to stimulate appetite. Just place ice into shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Hawthorne strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
