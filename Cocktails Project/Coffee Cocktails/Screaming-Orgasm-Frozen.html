Screaming Orgasm Frozen is yum yum above average cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Blended with 20ml hazelnut liqueur and 20ml coffee liqueur caf del mar with 20ml vodka 666 as well as 20ml whisky cream including 30ml ice cream also 60ml milk together with 70ml ice and best with buffet style food. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until silky smooth. Pour into glass tall poco glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
