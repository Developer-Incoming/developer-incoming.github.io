White Bat is delectable full bodied cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml rum light white bacardi and 15ml coffee liqueur kimberley rum with 35ml milk as well as 20ml cola soda including 190ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add cola soda to glass then add rum and layer milk. Add coffee liqueur and served in a highball glass cold.
