Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Vittorio Spirits
30 ml
Whisky Cream Sortilege
30 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur Soplica
30 ml
Cream
60 ml
Ice Cream
60 ml
Ice
14% Alcohol By Volume
28 Proof
2 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Put ice into blender.
  2. Pour all ingredients into cocktail blender.
  3. Blend until silky smooth.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Smaakvolle
Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl
