Ingredients
30 ml
Whisky Cream Irish Baileys
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Kahlua
20 ml
Cream Hot
Heat Cubes
Honey Joy
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
0.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Warm shaker and heat cubes.
  3. Add ingredients into warm shaker.
  4. Shake well until fluffy and warm.
  5. Hawthorne strain into glass.
  6. Serve honey joy on the side.
  7. Heavenly
Served
Shaken
Warm
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Joyful Anna is heavenly average cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml whisky cream irish baileys and 30ml coffee liqueur kahlua with 20ml cream hot and good with afternoon tea. Just warm glass then warm shaker and heat cubes and add ingredients into warm shaker. Shake well until fluffy and warm and hawthorne strain into glass and serve honey joy on the side and served in a sherry glass warm.
