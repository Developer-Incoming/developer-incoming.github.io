Ramsey is very palatable above average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml tequila blanco la cofradia and 15ml coffee liqueur decaf richardos with 15ml whisky cream as well as 55ml milk including 190ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a highball glass cold.
