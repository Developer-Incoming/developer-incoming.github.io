Ingredients

10 ml
Cognac
10 ml
Strega
10 ml
Rum Light White
30 ml
Coffee Brew
200 ml
Ice
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof

Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to glass.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Heavenly
Served

Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Camels Coffee is heavenly quite strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur tamborine mountain and 10ml cognac with 10ml strega as well as 10ml rum light white including 30ml coffee brew also 200ml ice and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just add ice to glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
