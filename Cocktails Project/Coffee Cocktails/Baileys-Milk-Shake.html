Baileys Milk Shake is drinkable smooth cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml whisky cream irish baileys and 15ml rum light white with 15ml coffee liqueur kamora as well as 60ml ice cream vanilla including 70ml milk also 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender, less creem and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and garnish with whipped cream and served in a highball glass cold.
