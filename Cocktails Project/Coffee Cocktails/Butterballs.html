Also See: Butterball

Butterballs is comestible full bodied cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml butterscotch schnapps vok and 30ml coffee liqueur with 20ml cream and best with buffet style food. Just add butterscotch schnapps to bottom of glass then float coffee liqueur and float cream and garnish with silver dust and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
