Honey Lemonade is stimulating watery mocktail with no alcohol. Stirred with 15ml lemon juice and 15ml honey with 70ml soda water as well as 190ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add ice to tall tumbler then pour all ingredients into mixing glass and stir well until mixed. Pour into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
