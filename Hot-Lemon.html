Hot Lemon is piquant watery mocktail with no alcohol. Stirred with 60ml lemon juice and 10ml sugar syrup with 50ml water hot and best served with fish and seafood dishes. Just heat glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and garnish with lemon peel and served in a martini glass warm.
