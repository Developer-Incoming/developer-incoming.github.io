Canadian Pride is luscious watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 10ml maple syrup and 60ml grapefruit juice with 40ml ginger ale soda as well as 290ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then add ice to shaker, less soda and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and top with ginger soda and served in a highball glass cold.
