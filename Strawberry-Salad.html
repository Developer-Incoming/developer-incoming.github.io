Strawberry Salad is bewitching watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 30ml strawberry pulp and 20ml apple juice with 20ml papaya juice as well as 20ml pineapple juice including 20ml soda water also 240ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker, less soda water. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and top with soda water and served in a poco grande glass cold.
