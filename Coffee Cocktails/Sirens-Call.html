Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add raspberries to glass.

120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Sirens Call is nummy full bodied cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur bebo cuban and 15ml raspberry liqueur teichenne with 15ml vodka as well as 100ml ice including 50ml raspberry and best with lunch. Just add raspberries to glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
