Ingredients
30 ml
Chocolate Liqueur Cream Beldoux
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Tia Maria
40 ml
Coffee Brew
190 ml
Ice
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Brew and chill coffee.
  2. Fill glass with ice.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Comestible
Served
Shaken
Cold
Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Coffee Chocolate is comestible average cocktail at 1 standard drinks.
