Dinner Espresso Martini is comestible robust cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml vodka vanilla and 15ml orange liqueur with 15ml coffee liqueur as well as 15ml amaretto including 15ml coffee brew also 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just brew coffee and chill then add ice to mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Mix well and strain into glass and served in a martini glass cold.
