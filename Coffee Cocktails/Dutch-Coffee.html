Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add Jenever gin to glass.
  4. Pour in warm coffee.
  5. Add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Good with a local pastry.
  8. Garnish with nutmeg.
  9. Heerlijke

Dutch Coffee is heerlijke standard cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Built with 60ml gin jenever and 190ml coffee brew and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and add jenever gin to glass. Pour in warm coffee and add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste then optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and good with a local pastry and garnish with nutmeg and served in a coffee glass warm.
