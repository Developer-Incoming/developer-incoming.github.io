Ambijaxtrious is yum yum strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Stirred with 20ml vodka citron ketel one and 20ml tequila coffee ponchos with 20ml coffee liqueur applewood as well as 40ml milk including 10ml grenadine also 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just fill glass with ice to chill then add ice to mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Stir well and strain into glass over ice and served in a martini glass cold.
