Hot Daring Dylan is lovely smooth cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml tequila anejo mala vida and 30ml coffee liqueur decaf richardos with 10ml chocolate powder as well as 80ml milk hot and best with lunch. Just place chocolate powder into glass then stir in hot milk and add tequila and coffee liqueur and float whipped cream on top and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
