Ingredients
40 ml
Cake Chocolate
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Tamborine Mountain
30 ml
Chocolate Liqueur
20 ml
Cheese Mascarpone
Cream Whipped
Chocolate Shavings
Cocktail Spork
11% Alcohol By Volume
22 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Cold
  1. Cut cake into a square then in half.
  2. Drizzle each half of cake with coffee liqueur.
  3. Place mascarpone between each cake.
  4. Add to cake to glass, split up.
  5. Pour chocolate liqueuer over cake to make a line.
  6. Serve with cocktail spoon.
  7. Garnish with whipped cream and chocolate powder.
  8. Thanks Nonni
Served
Layered
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Tiramisu is thanks nonni average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 40ml cake chocolate and 30ml coffee liqueur tamborine mountain with 30ml chocolate liqueur as well as 20ml cheese mascarpone and best with dessert type dishes. Just cut cake into a square then in half then drizzle each half of cake with coffee liqueur and place mascarpone between each cake. Add to cake to glass, split up and pour chocolate liqueuer over cake to make a line and serve with cocktail spoon and garnish with whipped cream and chocolate powder and served in a saucer glass cold.
