Eager Beaver is gourmande very strong cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum and 30ml coffee liqueur fair with 30ml orange liqueur as well as 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Pour all into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
