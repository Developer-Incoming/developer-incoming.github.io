Ingredients
60 ml
Coffee Liqueur Toussaint
190 ml
Coffee Brew
Chocolate Shavings
7% Alcohol By Volume
14 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add all ingredients to warm glass.
  4. Pour in warm coffee.
  5. Add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste.
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Garnish with sprinkle of chocolate shavings.
  8. Restoring
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl
