Toasted Sunrise is very enjoyable standard cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Layered with 45ml coffee liqueur and 15ml almond liqueur with 90ml milk and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just add coffee liqueur to glass then float milk over coffee and float almond liqueur on top of milk and garnish with slices roasted almonds and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
