Mile High Club is thirst quenching strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Built with 30ml vodka and 30ml coffee liqueur bebo cuban with 30ml cola soda as well as 10ml lime juice including 50ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just add a little ice to glass then add lime juice and pour in shot of vodka. Add a splash of cola soda and pour in shot coffee liqueur and add cola soda and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
