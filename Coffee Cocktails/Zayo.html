Ingredients
30 ml
Chocolate Liqueur Brown Flaschengeist
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Sabroso
10 ml
Bitters Chocolate ABC
10 ml
Bitters Coffee ABC
30 ml
Cream
Heat Cubes
Truffle Chocolate Coffee
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Warm shaker and heat cubes.
  3. Add all ingredients to warm shaker.
  4. Quick shake to mix.
  5. Hawthorne strain into glass.
  6. Serve with chocolate coffee truffle.
  7. Mouth Watering
Served
Shaken
Warm
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Zayo is mouth watering quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml chocolate liqueur brown flaschengeist and 30ml coffee liqueur sabroso with 10ml bitters chocolate abc as well as 10ml bitters coffee abc including 30ml cream and served for or with tiffins. Just warm glass then warm shaker and heat cubes and add all ingredients to warm shaker. Quick shake to mix and hawthorne strain into glass and serve with chocolate coffee truffle and served in a saucer glass warm.
