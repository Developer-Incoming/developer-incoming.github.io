16% Alcohol By Volume

Method - Layered - Room Temperature
  1. Add butterscotch liqueur into bottom of glass.
  2. Layer in whisky cream.
  3. Float coffee liqueur on top.
  4. Garnish top with whipped cream.
  5. Scrumptious

Layered
Room Temperature

Kellys Buttery Nipple is scrumptious full bodied cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml butterscotch liqueur and 20ml whisky cream with 20ml coffee liqueur also 20ml cream whipped and best with dessert type dishes. Just add butterscotch liqueur into bottom of glass then layer in whisky cream and float coffee liqueur on top and garnish top with whipped cream and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
