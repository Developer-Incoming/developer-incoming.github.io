Starlas Waver is fortifying full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml coffee cream timboon and 15ml rum hemp dead mans fingers with 15ml vodka hemp natural distilling as well as 45ml milk including 100ml ice and served with or for breakfast. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
