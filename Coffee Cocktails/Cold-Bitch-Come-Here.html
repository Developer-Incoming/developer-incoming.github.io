130 ml

Method - Blended - Cold

Cold Bitch Come Here is lovely very strong cocktail at 2.3 standard drinks. Blended with 25ml chocolate liqueur and 25ml raspberry liqueur with 20ml rum alta gama as well as 20ml coffee liqueur including 20ml butterscotch liqueur also 130ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just put ice into blender then pour all ingredients into blender and blend until smooth. Pour blended mixture into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
