Wild White Slippery Mudslide is pleasurable full bodied cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml whisky cream and 15ml coffee liqueur with 15ml butterscotch liqueur as well as 30ml cream including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just pour chocolate syrup around inside of glass then add ice to glass and pour in whisky cream. Add coffee liqueur and pour in cream and add butterscotch liqueur and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
