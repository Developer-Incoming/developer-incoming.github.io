Ingredients
25 ml
Rum Light White
25 ml
Creme de Cacao Brown McGuinness
10 ml
Almond Liqueur
190 ml
Coffee Brew
6% Alcohol By Volume
12 Proof
1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Heat glass.
  2. Add white rum to warm glass.
  3. Add brown creme de cacao.
  4. Add warm coffee.
  5. Float almond liqueur on top.
  6. Add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste.
  7. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  8. Bracing
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

White Caypso Coffee is bracing weak cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Built with 25ml rum light white and 25ml creme de cacao brown mcguinness with 10ml almond liqueur as well as 190ml coffee brew and best when a coffee is needed. Just heat glass then add white rum to warm glass and add brown creme de cacao. Add warm coffee and float almond liqueur on top and add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
