Eitan is velsmagende quite strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur keuck mokka and 30ml rum spiced soul with 30ml cola soda as well as 100ml ice and best at tea time. Just add ice to glass then add coffee liqueur to glass and pour in shot of spiced rum. Top with cola soda and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
