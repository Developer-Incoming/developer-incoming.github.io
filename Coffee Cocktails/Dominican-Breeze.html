Dominican Breeze is reviving heavy cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Built with 45ml rum brugal extra viejo and 15ml coffee liqueur mokatika with 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just fill glass with ice then add rum to glass and add coffee liqueur and served in a old fashioned glass .
