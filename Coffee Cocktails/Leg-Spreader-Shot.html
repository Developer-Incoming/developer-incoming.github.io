Leg Spreader Shot is delightful average cocktail at 0.5 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml coffee liqueur celeste noir and 30ml sambuca ramazzotti and best with lunch. Just two shots always share a shot then add coffee liqueur and float sambuca on top and served in a shot glass cold.
