Ingredients
30 ml
Vodka Hemp Mystic
15 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur
15 ml
Sugar Syrup
50 ml
Coffee Brew
100 ml
Ice
14% Alcohol By Volume
28 Proof
1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Brew and chill coffee.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Palatable
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Mystic Night is palatable full bodied cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml vodka hemp mystic and 15ml hazelnut liqueur with 15ml sugar syrup as well as 50ml coffee brew including 100ml ice and works well with savoury style foods. Just brew and chill coffee then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
