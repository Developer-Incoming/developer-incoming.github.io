30 ml

10 ml

Ice
20% Alcohol By Volume

Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Savoureux
Served
Shaken

Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Brandy Snap is savoureux quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml brandy and 30ml sherry dry with 40ml orange juice as well as 10ml bitters coffee abc including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
