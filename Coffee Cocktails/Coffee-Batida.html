Ingredients
30 ml
Cachaca Cana Rio
30 ml
Coffee Brew
20 ml
Cream
10 ml
Sugar Syrup
100 ml
Ice
13% Alcohol By Volume
26 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Fill glass with ice cubes.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Delish
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Coffee Batida is delish above average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml cachaca cana rio and 30ml coffee brew with 20ml cream as well as 10ml sugar syrup including 100ml ice and served with or for breakfast. Just fill glass with ice cubes then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
