Big Toe is moreish very strong cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Stirred with 10ml hazelnut liqueur and 10ml almond liqueur with 10ml coffee liqueur copa de oro as well as 10ml whisky cream including 10ml rum light white expedition also 10ml creme de cacao brown and best with lunch. Just add ingredients to mixing glass then stir well and strain into glass and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
