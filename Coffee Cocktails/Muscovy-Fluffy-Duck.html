Ingredients
30 ml
Tequila Gold
30 ml
Advocaat Warninks
30 ml
Cream
10 ml
Orange Juice
100 ml
Ice
Grenadine
Coffee Liqueur
16% Alcohol By Volume
32 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients.
  3. Shake well until foaming.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Garnish with grenadine and a blob of coffee liqueur.
  6. Delectable Cairina Moschata
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Muscovy Fluffy Duck is delectable cairina moschata full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml tequila gold and 30ml advocaat warninks with 30ml cream as well as 10ml orange juice including 100ml ice and best with egg dishes. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients and shake well until foaming. Strain into glass and garnish with grenadine and a blob of coffee liqueur and served in a saucer glass cold.
