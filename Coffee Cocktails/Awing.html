Awing is geschmackvollen quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur bebo cuban and 15ml whisky cream with 15ml orange liqueur mcguinness as well as 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just pack ice into glass then pour over ice coffee liqueur and along one side add orange liqueur. On the other side pour in whisky cream and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
