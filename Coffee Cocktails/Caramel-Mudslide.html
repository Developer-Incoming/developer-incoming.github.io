Vodka Caramel Hunter
100 ml

Cocoa Powder
25% Alcohol By Volume
50 Proof
1.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Pour in shot of coffee liqueur.
  3. Add shot of caramel vodka.
  4. Pour whisky cream.
  5. Flick a little cocoa powder on top.
  6. Wohlschmeckende
Served
Poured
Cold

Caramel Mudslide is wohlschmeckende robust cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Built with 30ml whisky cream whistler and 30ml coffee liqueur kahlua with 30ml vodka caramel hunter as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then pour in shot of coffee liqueur and add shot of caramel vodka. Pour whisky cream and flick a little cocoa powder on top and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
