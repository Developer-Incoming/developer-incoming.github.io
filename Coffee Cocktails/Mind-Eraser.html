Mind Eraser is lovely robust cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Built with 30ml vodka and 30ml coffee liqueur flor de cana with 30ml tonic water as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then add vodka to glass and pour in coffee liqueur. Top with tonic and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
