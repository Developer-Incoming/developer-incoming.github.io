Ingredients
30 ml
Galliano Vanilla
30 ml
Tequila Reposado Cazcabel
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Jagermeister
Heat Cubes
34% Alcohol By Volume
68 Proof
2.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Warm
  1. Heat old short glass.
  2. Warm shaker and heat cubes.
  3. Add all ingredients to warm shaker.
  4. Shake well until warm.
  5. Fine strain into warm glass.
  6. Scrummy

Sleeper: a person who is asleep

Served
Shaken
Warm
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl
Also See: Hard Nightcap

Sleeper is scrummy heavy cocktail at 2.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml galliano vanilla and 30ml tequila reposado cazcabel with 30ml coffee liqueur jagermeister and taken as nightcap before bedtime. Just heat old short glass then warm shaker and heat cubes and add all ingredients to warm shaker. Shake well until warm and fine strain into warm glass and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
