Ingredients
60 ml
Coffee Liqueur Cream Somrus
30 ml
Whiskey Irish Redbreast 12year
100 ml
Ice
22% Alcohol By Volume
44 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake to mix.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Top glass with more ice.
  6. Delectable
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Irish Coffee Express is delectable quite strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 60ml coffee liqueur cream somrus and 30ml whiskey irish redbreast 12year with 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour everything into glass and top glass with more ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
