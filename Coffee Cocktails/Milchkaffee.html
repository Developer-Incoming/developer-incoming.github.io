Milchkaffee is geschmackvollen watered down cocktail at 0.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee cream timboon and 30ml coffee brew with 190ml milk hot and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and steam milk and add shot of coffee. Pour in coffee liqueur to glass and pour in warm steamed milk and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
