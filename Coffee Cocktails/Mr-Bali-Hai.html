10 ml

10 ml

25 ml

100 ml
Ice

Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake.

Mr Bali Hai is bracing full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum black and 15ml coffee liqueur coldpresso with 10ml fassionola gold syrup as well as 10ml lemon juice including 25ml pineapple juice also 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
