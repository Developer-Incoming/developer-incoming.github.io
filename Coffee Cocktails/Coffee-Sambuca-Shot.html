Ingredients
30 ml
Sambuca Molinari
Coffee Bean
40% Alcohol By Volume
80 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. TWO SHOTS - Always Share A Shot :)
  2. Warm glass.
  3. Pour sambuca into glass.
  4. Top with three coffee bean.
  5. Light glass.
  6. Burn until beans crack.
  7. Blow out fire.
  8. Tasteful Shot Shooter
Served On Fire
Poured
Warm
Shot Glass
30ml, 1oz, 3cl
Also See: Shooter Shot Flaming Drink

Coffee Sambuca Shot is tasteful full strength cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml sambuca molinari and best when a coffee is needed. Just two shots always share a shot then warm glass and pour sambuca into glass. Top with three coffee bean and light glass and burn until beans crack then blow out fire and served in a shot glass warm.
