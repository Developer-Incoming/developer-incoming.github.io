Ingredients
15 ml
Rum
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur
15 ml
Chocolate Liqueur
15 ml
Butterscotch Liqueur
15 ml
Raspberry Liqueur
100 ml
Ice
27% Alcohol By Volume
54 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Top glass with ice.
  6. Delightful
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass

Hey Bitch Come Here is delightful very strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml rum and 15ml coffee liqueur with 15ml chocolate liqueur as well as 15ml butterscotch liqueur including 15ml raspberry liqueur also 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
