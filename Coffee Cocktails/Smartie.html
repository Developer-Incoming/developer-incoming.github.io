Smartie is delectable full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml chocolate liqueur marie brizard and 30ml coffee liqueur with 30ml orange juice and served with sweet dishes. Just place clear chocolate liqueur into glass then float coffee liquer on top and float orange juice and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
