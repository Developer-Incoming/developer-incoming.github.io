Method - Layered - Room Temperature

  2. Add coffee liqueur to bottom of glass.
  3. Float whisky cream.
  4. Float triple sec on top.
  5. Light triple sec on fire.
  6. Freshening

Flaming B52 is freshening very strong cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml coffee liqueur applewood and 30ml whisky cream irish merrys with 30ml orange liqueur cointreau and best with lunch. Just warm glass then add coffee liqueur to bottom of glass and float whisky cream. Float triple sec on top and light triple sec on fire and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
