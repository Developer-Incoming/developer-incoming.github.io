ABC Sledge Hammer is stimulating heavy cocktail at 2.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 25ml brandy apple and 25ml brandy blackcurrant with 25ml brandy coffee as well as 15ml sugar syrup including 100ml ice and best at tea time. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and vigorously shake. Pour everything into glass and fill glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
