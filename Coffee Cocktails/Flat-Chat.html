Flat Chat is dainty full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Built with 30ml rum coffee dead mans fingers and 10ml bitters quince with 60ml cola soda as well as 190ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just place ice into tall glass then splash in quince bitters and pour in shot of coffee rum. Top with cola soda and served in a highball glass cold.
