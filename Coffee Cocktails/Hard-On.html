Hard On is succulent full bodied cocktail at 0.7 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur tia maria and 20ml banana liqueur with 20ml cream and best with buffet style food. Just two shots always share a shot then add coffee liqueur to bottom of glass and float banana liqueur. Float cream on top and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
