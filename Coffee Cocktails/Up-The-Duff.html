15 ml

15 ml

35 ml
Cream

Ice Cream
Cocktail Spoon
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Use cold glass.
  2. Add scoop of ice cream to glass.
  3. Add one ice cube to shaker.
  4. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  5. Quick wip shake.
  6. Pour into glass over ice cream.
  7. Serve with cocktail spoon.
  8. Energizing

Up The Duff is energizing average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml brandy oude meester and 15ml coffee liqueur heering with 15ml chocolate liqueur as well as 35ml cream including 30ml ice cream and served with sweet dishes. Just use cold glass then add scoop of ice cream to glass and add one ice cube to shaker. Add all ingredients to shaker and quick wip shake and pour into glass over ice cream and serve with cocktail spoon and served in a saucer glass cold.
