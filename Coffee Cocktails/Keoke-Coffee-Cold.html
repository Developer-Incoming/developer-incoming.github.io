Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Brew
20 ml
Brandy
20 ml
Coffee Liqueur
20 ml
Creme de Cacao Brown Giffard
100 ml
Ice
10 ml
Cream
18% Alcohol By Volume
36 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Warm
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Garnish with cream.
  6. Succulent
Served
Shaken
Warm
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Keoke Coffee Cold is succulent strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee brew and 20ml brandy with 20ml coffee liqueur as well as 20ml creme de cacao brown giffard including 100ml ice also 10ml cream and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and garnish with cream and served in a saucer glass warm.
