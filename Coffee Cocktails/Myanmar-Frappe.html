Myanmar Frappe is invigorating strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml vodka black blavod and 30ml chocolate liqueur brown briottet with 10ml vanilla syrup as well as 30ml coffee brew including 190ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just fill glass with ice then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Pour into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
