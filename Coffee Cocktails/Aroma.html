120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Aroma is nectareous quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml rum and 15ml coffee liqueur with 30ml coconut cream as well as 10ml sugar syrup including 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and vigorously shake. Strain into glass and garnish with a dusting of chocolate powder and served in a saucer glass cold.
