Ingredients
15 ml

15 ml
Bourbon Springbank
15 ml
Vodka
15 ml
Cinnamon Liqueur
100 ml
Ice
30% Alcohol By Volume
60 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Quick shake.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Add more ice to glass.

Mint Condition is heavenly very strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml coffee liqueur kavalan and 15ml bourbon springbank with 15ml vodka as well as 15ml cinnamon liqueur including 100ml ice and best at tea time. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and quick shake. Pour everything into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
