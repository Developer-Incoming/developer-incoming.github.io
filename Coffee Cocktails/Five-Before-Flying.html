Five Before Flying is delicious standard cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml bourbon and 15ml brandy with 15ml whisky yamazaki 12year as well as 15ml chocolate liqueur brown including 15ml banana liqueur also 30ml cream together with 145ml coffee bean and best with lunch. Just warm glass and brew coffee then add all spirits into glass and stir in the coffee. Float cream on top and served in a coffee glass warm.
