Ingredients
30 ml

30 ml

30 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice
Doughnut Hazelnut
22% Alcohol By Volume
44 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Add more ice to lowball glass.
  6. Good with warm hazelnut doughnut.
  7. Tasteful
Served
Shaken
Cold

Kioki Coffee is tasteful quite strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml brandy and 30ml coffee liqueur ambra with 30ml cream as well as 100ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Pour all into glass and add more ice to lowball glass and good with warm hazelnut doughnut and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
