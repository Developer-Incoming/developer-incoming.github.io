Saikkosen Special is flavoursome very strong cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml coffee liqueur tia maria and 30ml whisky cream irish baileys with 30ml orange liqueur cointreau and best with buffet style food. Just pour coffee liqueur into bottom of glass then float whisky crem on top and float orange liqueur and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
