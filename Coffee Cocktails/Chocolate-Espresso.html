Chocolate Espresso is luscious very strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml vodka chocolate carvo and 15ml coffee liqueur giffard with 15ml creme de cacao as well as 15ml coffee liqueur mr black including 10ml bitters chocolate spiced also 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and served in a martini glass cold.
