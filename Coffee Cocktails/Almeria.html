Method - Shaken - Cold

Almeria is moreish robust cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml rum dark and 15ml coffee liqueur kamora with 10ml egg white as well as 20ml lemon juice including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add all ingredients to shaker then dry shake without ice and add ice to shaker. Shake again until foamy and strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
