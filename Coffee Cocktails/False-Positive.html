15 ml

30 ml

100 ml
Ice

Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Quick shake.

200ml, 7oz, 20cl

False Positive is delectable robust cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml coffee liqueur misceo and 15ml rum coconut belmont estate with 30ml cream as well as 100ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and quick shake. Pour everything into glass and fill glass with more ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
