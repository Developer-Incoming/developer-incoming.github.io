Simple Charm is delicious quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 25ml cognac meukow and 25ml coffee liqueur tia maria dark with 25ml amaretto as well as 25ml cream including 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just place ice into shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Fine strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
