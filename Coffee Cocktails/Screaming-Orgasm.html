Screaming Orgasm is finger licking quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml amaretto and 30ml whisky cream with 15ml coffee liqueur as well as 15ml vodka including 10ml milk also 10ml cream together with 100ml ice and best with an entree or small snack. Just place ice into mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well until mixed. Hawthorne strain into glass and served in a martini glass cold.
