Paralyzer is succulent average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 15ml vodka and 15ml coffee liqueur belle isle with 60ml cola soda as well as 10ml cream whipped including 190ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just fill glass with ice then add vodka and coffee liqueur and pour in cola soda. Top with whipped cream and garnish with maraschino cherry and served in a highball glass cold.
