Ingredients
40 ml
Rum Gold Amber Bacardi
10 ml
Banana Liqueur
10 ml
Coffee Liqueur
10 ml
Lime Juice
20 ml
Pineapple Juice
200 ml
Ice
23% Alcohol By Volume
46 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Place ice into shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Inspiriting
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Shite Sands is inspiriting robust cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 40ml rum gold amber bacardi and 10ml banana liqueur with 10ml coffee liqueur as well as 10ml lime juice including 20ml pineapple juice also 200ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then place ice into shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
