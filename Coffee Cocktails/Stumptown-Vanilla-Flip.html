Ingredients
30 ml
Vanilla Liqueur
30 ml
Orange Liqueur Angel d Or
30 ml
Coffee Brew
10 ml
Egg White
100 ml
Ice
19% Alcohol By Volume
38 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Brew and chill coffee.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Dry shake without ice.
  4. Place ice into shaker.
  5. Shake well.
  6. Strain into glass.
  7. Enjoyable
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl
Also See: Flip

Stumptown Vanilla Flip is enjoyable strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml vanilla liqueur and 30ml orange liqueur angel d or with 30ml coffee brew as well as 10ml egg white including 100ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just brew and chill coffee then add all ingredients to shaker and dry shake without ice. Place ice into shaker and shake well and strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
