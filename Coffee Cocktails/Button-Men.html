30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Caf Del Mar
15 ml
Whisky Nant Sherry Cask
45 ml
Milk
100 ml
Ice
17% Alcohol By Volume
34 Proof
1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Put ice cubes into shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Vigorously shake.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Top glass with ice.
  6. Delicioso

Shaken

Button Men is delicioso strong cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur caf del mar and 15ml whisky nant sherry cask with 45ml milk as well as 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just put ice cubes into shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and vigorously shake. Pour all into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
