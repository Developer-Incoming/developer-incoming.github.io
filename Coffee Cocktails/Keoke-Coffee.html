Method - Poured - Warm

  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add all ingredients to glass.
  4. Pour in warm coffee.

Keoke Coffee is comestible mild cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 25ml brandy grape armagnac janneau and 25ml coffee liqueur keuck mokka with 10ml chocolate liqueur bols as well as 190ml coffee brew and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and add all ingredients to glass. Pour in warm coffee and add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
