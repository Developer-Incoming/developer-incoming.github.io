Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffe and heat milk.
  3. Add coffee liqueur to glass.
  4. Pour in vodka.
  5. Add brewed coffe.
  6. Pour in warm milk.
  7. Add sugar, cream, etc to taste.
  8. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  9. Appetitoso Cafe Latte

Caffe Latte is appetitoso cafe latte light in flavour cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Built with 15ml coffee liqueur luxe brew and 15ml vodka coffee hunter with 30ml coffee brew as well as 190ml milk hot and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffe and heat milk and add coffee liqueur to glass. Pour in vodka and add brewed coffe and pour in warm milk then add sugar, cream, etc to taste and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
