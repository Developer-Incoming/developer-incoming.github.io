Method - Shaken - Cold

200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Bourbon Fog is dainty full bodied cocktail at 2.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 60ml bourbon knob creek and 60ml coffee with 60ml ice cream and best with lunch. Just brew and chill glass then use chilled glass and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well until all is well mixed and pour into ice cold glass and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
