Bandits Coffee is bewitching robust cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Built with 30ml tequila reposado and 30ml coffee liqueur merlet with 30ml cola soda as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to glass then add gold tequila and add coffee liqueur. Top with cola soda and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
