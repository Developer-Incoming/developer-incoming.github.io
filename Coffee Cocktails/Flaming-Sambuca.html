Flaming Sambuca is flavourful full strength cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Built with 60ml sambuca luxardo and best served with fish and seafood dishes. Just warm glass then two shots - always share a shot :) and add sambuca to glass. Top with three coffee beans and ignite wait until the beans crackle blow out drink and served in a shot glass warm.
