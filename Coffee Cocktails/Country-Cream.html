Country Cream is very enjoyable standard cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Blended with 20ml coffee liqueur and 20ml pear liqueur cream lauria alpine with 10ml capsicum liqueur as well as 10ml raspberry liqueur distillery botanica including 60ml ice cream vanilla also 120ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
