90ml, 3oz, 9cl

My American Dream is dainty robust cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml coffee liqueur and 15ml almond liqueur with 15ml hazelnut liqueur giffard as well as 15ml creme de cacao brown including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and served in a sherry glass cold.
