Wake A Lazy Dog is invigorating average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee schnapps and 30ml raspberry schnapps with 50ml cola soda as well as 190ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to high ball glass then pour in coffee schnapps and add raspberry schnapps. Top with cola soda and served in a highball glass cold.
