Mozartus is scrumptious full bodied cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 25ml chocolate liqueur mozart and 25ml whisky cream with 10ml coffee liqueur lexington hill as well as 100ml ice and served before a meal to stimulate appetite. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
