Ingredients
15 ml
Chocolate Schnapps Vizor
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Marie Brizard
30 ml
Vanilla Liqueur Kahlua
200 ml
Ice
20% Alcohol By Volume
40 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Add ice to mixing glass.
  3. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  4. Stir well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Palatable
Served
Stirred
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Iced Mocha is palatable quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml chocolate schnapps vizor and 15ml coffee liqueur marie brizard with 30ml vanilla liqueur kahlua as well as 200ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just pack ice into glass then add ice to mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Stir well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
