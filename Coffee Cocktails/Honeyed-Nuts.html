Ingredients
30 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur
10 ml
Advocaat
30 ml
Cream
10 ml
Honey
100 ml
Ice
Hazelnut
15% Alcohol By Volume
30 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Garnish with crushed hazelunts.
  6. Ambrosian
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Honeyed Nuts is ambrosian full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml hazelnut liqueur and 30ml coffee liqueur with 10ml advocaat as well as 30ml cream including 10ml honey also 100ml ice and served before a meal to stimulate appetite. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and garnish with crushed hazelunts and served in a saucer glass cold.
