Ingredients
30 ml
Ice Cream Chocolate
30 ml
Ice Cream Coffee
30 ml
Chocolate Liqueur DeKuyper
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Pietro Gallo
Pistachio
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Chill glass.
  2. Quenelle chocolate ice cream into glass.
  3. Quenelle coffee ice cream into glass.
  4. Pour chocolate liqueur over chocolate ice cream.
  5. Pour coffee liqueur over coffee ice cream.
  6. Garnish with chopped pistacios.
  7. Beguiling
Served
Poured
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl
