Heart Throb is drinkable digestive strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml coffee liqueur bols and 30ml cream with 30ml rum black and best with an entree or small snack. Just add coffee to bottom of glass then float cream and float black rum. Pousse-Cafe and good to have with dark chocolate truffle and served in a sherry glass cold.
