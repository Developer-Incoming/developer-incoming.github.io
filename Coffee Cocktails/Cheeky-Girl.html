Cheeky Girl is dainty full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml rum coconut cream brinley shipwreck and 15ml cognac esteve with 15ml banana liqueur as well as 15ml coffee liqueur bvland including 30ml cream also 100ml ice and served before a meal to stimulate appetite. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and top with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
