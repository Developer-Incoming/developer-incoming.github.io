Mulata Daiquiri is lovely strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml rum light white and 15ml coffee liqueur vok with 30ml lime juice as well as 20ml sugar syrup including 100ml ice and served with or for breakfast. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
