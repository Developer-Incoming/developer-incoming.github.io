Ingredients
30 ml
Vodka Thunder Toffee
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur
60 ml
Ice Cream Vanilla
120 ml
Ice
Chocolate Shavings
14% Alcohol By Volume
28 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Add ice to blender.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour blended mixture into glass.
  5. Garnish with chocolate shavings.
  6. Nummy
Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Hammer Horror is nummy full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml vodka thunder toffee and 30ml coffee liqueur with 60ml ice cream vanilla as well as 120ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until smooth. Pour blended mixture into glass and garnish with chocolate shavings and served in a poco grande glass cold.
