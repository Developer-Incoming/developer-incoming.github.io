Papal Army is cuore antipasto full bodied cocktail at 0.7 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml amaro antico and 15ml chocolate liqueur brown tempus fugit with 30ml coffee brew and best when a coffee is needed. Just brew strong short espresso coffee then warm glass and add amaro and chocolate liqueur into glass. Pour in warm shot espresso coffeee and always good with hot overn brioche and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
