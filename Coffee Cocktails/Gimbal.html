Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Two Brothers
30 ml
Rum Vanilla Admiral Nelson
30 ml
Evaporated Milk
100 ml
Ice
18% Alcohol By Volume
36 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add ingredients into shaker over ice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Top glass with ice.
  6. Freshening
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Gimbal is freshening strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur two brothers and 30ml rum vanilla admiral nelson with 30ml evaporated milk as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add ingredients into shaker over ice and shake well. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
