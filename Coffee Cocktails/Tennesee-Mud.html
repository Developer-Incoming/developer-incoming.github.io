Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm coffee glass.
  2. Bew coffee.
  3. Pour in shot of tennessee whisky.
  4. Add amaretto to glass.
  5. Pour in coffee.
  6. Add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste.
  7. Best serving temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  8. Delightful

Tennesee Mud is delightful smooth cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Built with 30ml whiskey rollins and 30ml amaretto with 190ml coffee brew and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm coffee glass then bew coffee and pour in shot of tennessee whisky. Add amaretto to glass and pour in coffee and add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste then best serving temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and served in a coffee glass warm.
