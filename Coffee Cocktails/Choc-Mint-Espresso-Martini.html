Choc Mint Espresso Martini is succulent robust cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Stirred with 45ml gin and 15ml chocolate liqueur brown bols with 15ml mint liqueur mocha as well as 15ml coffee brew including 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and garnish with mint and served in a martini glass cold.
