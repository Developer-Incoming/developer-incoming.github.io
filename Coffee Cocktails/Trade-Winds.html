Trade Winds: a wind blowing steadily towards the equator

Served
Stirred
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Trade Winds is inviting quite strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml bourbon bearded lady and 10ml bitters suze red with 5ml amaro averna as well as 5ml bitters chocolate including 10ml sugar syrup also 30ml coffee brew together with 100ml ice and best at tea time. Just brew and chill coffee then add half the ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Quick shake and pour all into glass and top glass with ice cubes and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
