Coach Shooter is heavenly robust cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur lawrenny and 15ml almond liqueur with 15ml chocolate liqueur bols and best with buffet style food. Just two shots always share a shot then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
