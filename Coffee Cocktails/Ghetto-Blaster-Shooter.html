Ghetto Blaster Shooter is appealing heavy cocktail at 2.2 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur copa de oro and 20ml brandy stock with 20ml tequila as well as 20ml whisky rye and best with an entree or small snack. Just two shots always share a shot then pour coffee liqueur into glass and layer in brandy. Float in tequila and float rye whiskey on top and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
