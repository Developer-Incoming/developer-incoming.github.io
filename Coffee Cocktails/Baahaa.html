Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Quick Brown Fox
30 ml
Chocolate Liqueur Brown
30 ml
Milo Powder
160 ml
Milk Hot
Doughnut Milo
5% Alcohol By Volume
10 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Pour in shot of coffee liquor.
  3. Add shot of chocolate liquor.
  4. Add milo powder.
  5. Pour in warm milk and stir until well mixed.
  6. Serve with milo donut.
  7. Happy Morning
Served
Stirred
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Baahaa is happy morning light cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee liqueur quick brown fox and 30ml chocolate liqueur brown with 30ml milo powder as well as 160ml milk hot and served for or with tiffins. Just warm glass then pour in shot of coffee liquor and add shot of chocolate liquor. Add milo powder and pour in warm milk and stir until well mixed and serve with milo donut and served in a coffee glass warm.
