Shamrock Shaker is delicious average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml coffee liqueur iseo and 15ml almond liqueur with 50ml milk as well as 100ml ice and served before a meal to stimulate appetite. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and garnish green and gold dust powder and served in a saucer glass cold.
