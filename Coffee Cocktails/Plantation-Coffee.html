Method - Shaken - Cold

  3. Add ice to shaker.

Plantation Coffee is nummy full bodied cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum gold amber angostura bfly anejo and 15ml orange liqueur with 15ml creme de cacao brown as well as 40ml coffee brew including 20ml cream also 130ml ice and best with lunch. Just brew and chill coffee then pack ice into glass and add ice to shaker. Add all ingredients to shaker and shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
