Ingredients
30 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur
30 ml
Whisky Cream
15 ml
Banana Liqueur
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur
30 ml
Banana Pulp
30 ml
Coffee Brew
10 ml
Chocolate Syrup
80 ml
Ice
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Brew coffe and chill
  2. Put ice into blender.
  3. Add all ingredients to blender.
  4. Blend until very smooth.
  5. Pour into glass.
Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Sabuzzo is pour into glass average cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml hazelnut liqueur and 30ml whisky cream with 15ml banana liqueur as well as 15ml coffee liqueur including 30ml banana pulp also 30ml coffee brew together with 10ml chocolate syrup and 80ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just brew coffe and chill then put ice into blender and add all ingredients to blender. Blend until very smooth and served in a poco grande glass cold.
