30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Dolce
30 ml
Whisky Cream
10 ml
Butterscotch Liqueur
100 ml
Ice
18% Alcohol By Volume
36 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Fill glass with ice.
  2. Add coffee liqueur.
  3. Pour in whisky cream.
  4. Flick a little butterscotch liqueur on top.
  5. Palatable

Cormoran: a rather large diving bird

Served
Poured
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass

Also See: Cormoran

Cormoran is palatable strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur dolce and 30ml whisky cream with 10ml butterscotch liqueur as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just fill glass with ice then add coffee liqueur and pour in whisky cream and flick a little butterscotch liqueur on top and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
