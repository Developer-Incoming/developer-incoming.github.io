Mexican Colada is scrumptious full bodied cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Blended with 45ml tequila reposado solento and 15ml coffee liqueur with 40ml pineapple juice as well as 40ml coconut cream including 100ml ice and best with morning tea snacks. Just add ice to blender then pour all ingredients into bar blender and blend until silky smooth. Pour blended mixture into glass and served in a poco grande glass cold.
