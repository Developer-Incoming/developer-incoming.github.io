Ingredients
40 ml
Rum Gold Amber
10 ml
Orange Liqueur Cello
10 ml
Coffee Liqueur
10 ml
Grenadine
10 ml
Lime Juice
200 ml
Ice
26% Alcohol By Volume
52 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Add ice to mixing glass.
  3. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  4. Stir well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Freshening
Served
Stirred
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Centenario is freshening very strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Stirred with 40ml rum gold amber and 10ml orange liqueur cello with 10ml coffee liqueur as well as 10ml grenadine including 10ml lime juice also 200ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then add ice to mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Stir well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
