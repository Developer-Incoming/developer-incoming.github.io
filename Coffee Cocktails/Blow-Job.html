Blow Job is flavorous strong cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Stirred with 20ml orange liqueur and 20ml coffee liqueur giffard with 20ml banana liqueur gabriel boudier as well as 20ml cream whipped and best with dessert type dishes. Just add ingredients to mixing glass then stir well and pour into glass. Top with whipped cream and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
