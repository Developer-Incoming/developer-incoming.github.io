30 ml
Orange Liqueur
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Tanglin
15 ml
Cognac
200 ml
Ice
26% Alcohol By Volume
52 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Interagir
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Velvet Hammer is interagir very strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml orange liqueur and 30ml coffee liqueur tanglin with 15ml cognac as well as 200ml ice and best with lunch. Just pack ice into glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
