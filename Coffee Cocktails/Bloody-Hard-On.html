Bloody Hard On is succulent strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur mokatika and 20ml strawberry liqueur suntory with 20ml whisky cream and best with buffet style food. Just two shots always share a shot then add coffee to bottom of glass and float strawberry liqueur. Float whisky cream on top and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
