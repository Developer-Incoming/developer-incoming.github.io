Viking Warmer is tasty quite strong cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Built with 45ml vodka coffee whisky jumping goat and 15ml akvavit with 10ml blueberry syrup as well as 10ml lime juice including 20ml lemon soda also 100ml ice and taken after dinner or late in the night. Just pack ice into glass then pour ingredients over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
