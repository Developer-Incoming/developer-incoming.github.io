90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Landslide is exquisite quite strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml apricot liqueur and 20ml coffee liqueur with 20ml banana liqueur as well as 20ml whisky cream and best with an entree or small snack. Just place layer of apricot liqueur into bottom of glass then layer in coffee liquer and add layer of banana liqueur. Top with layer of whisky cream and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
