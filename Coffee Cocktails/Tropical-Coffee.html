Ingredients
60 ml
Rum Gold Amber Havana Club
190 ml
Coffee Brew
Sugarcane
Pastry Danish Fruit Mixed
10% Alcohol By Volume
20 Proof
2 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add all ingredients to warm glass.
  4. Pour in warm coffee.
  5. Add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste.
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Good with a tropical fruit danish.
  8. Mouth Watering
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Tropical Coffee is mouth watering standard cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Built with 60ml rum gold amber havana club and 190ml coffee brew and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and add all ingredients to warm glass. Pour in warm coffee and add sugar, milk, cream or citrus, etc to taste and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and good with a tropical fruit danish and served in a coffee glass warm.
