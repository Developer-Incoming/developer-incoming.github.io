Ingredients
30 ml
Butterscotch Liqueur
15 ml
Whisky Cream
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur
30 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice
Dust Brown
15% Alcohol By Volume
30 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Sprinkle a little brown sugar dust on top.
  6. Wohlschmeckende
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl
Also See: Sugar Daddy

Sugar Daddy is wohlschmeckende full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml butterscotch liqueur and 15ml whisky cream with 15ml coffee liqueur as well as 30ml cream including 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and sprinkle a little brown sugar dust on top and served in a saucer glass cold.
