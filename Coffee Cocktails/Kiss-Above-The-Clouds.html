Kiss Above The Clouds is bewitching heavy cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml rum matusalem and 15ml whisky with 15ml vermouth sweet as well as 15ml coffee liqueur belle isle including 10ml bitters chocolate spiced also 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
