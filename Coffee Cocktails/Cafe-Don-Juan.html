Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur
30 ml
Rum Black Captain Morgan
190 ml
Coffee Brew
Chocolate Shavings
8% Alcohol By Volume
16 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add sugar rim to glass.
  4. Add all ingredients to glass.
  5. Pour in warm coffee.
  6. Add sugar, milk, cream, etc to taste.
  7. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  8. Garnish with chocolate shavings.
  9. Divine
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl
