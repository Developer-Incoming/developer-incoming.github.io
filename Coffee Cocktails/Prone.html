Ingredients
30 ml
Macadamia Liqueur Kahana
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Merry Widow
30 ml
Milk
100 ml
Ice
15% Alcohol By Volume
30 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Add macagamia liqueur to glass.
  3. Pour in coffee liqueur.
  4. Add milk.
  5. Stir a little.
  6. Invigorating

Prone: lying flat especially face downwards

Served
Poured
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Prone is invigorating full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml macadamia liqueur kahana and 30ml coffee liqueur merry widow with 30ml milk as well as 100ml ice and best with dessert type dishes. Just pack ice into glass then add macagamia liqueur to glass and pour in coffee liqueur. Add milk and stir a little and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
