Ingredients
60 ml
Orujo Olave
10 ml
Sugar Syrup
Orange Peel
Coffee Bean
34% Alcohol By Volume
68 Proof
1.9 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Pour sugar syrup into bottom of glass.
  3. Add in orange peel.
  4. Pour in orujo.
  5. Add coffee beans into glass.
  6. Ignite alcohol and serve.
  7. Lovely
Served On Fire
Poured
Warm
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Queimada is lovely heavy cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Built with 60ml orujo olave and 10ml sugar syrup and best with buffet style food. Just warm glass then pour sugar syrup into bottom of glass and add in orange peel. Pour in orujo and add coffee beans into glass and ignite alcohol and serve and served in a sherry glass warm.
