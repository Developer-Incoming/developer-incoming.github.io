Papa Muminek is bewitching average cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Built with 15ml whisky yamazaki and 15ml advocaat with 15ml chocolate liqueur as well as 15ml coffee liqueur including 15ml banana liqueur also 15ml apple juice together with 80ml milk and 130ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just place half the ice to glass then add other half of the ice into shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Vigorously shake and pour everything into glass and add more ice to glass and served in a goblet glass cold.
