Ingredients
30 ml
Almond Liqueur
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Galliano
15 ml
Milk
15 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice
Almond
18% Alcohol By Volume
36 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Toasted almond until, yummy level.
  2. Add ice to glass.
  3. Add all ingredients.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Garnish with toasted almond.
  7. Mouth Watering
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Toasted Almond is mouth watering strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml almond liqueur and 30ml coffee liqueur galliano with 15ml milk as well as 15ml cream including 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just toasted almond until, yummy level then add ice to glass and add all ingredients. Shake well and strain into glass and garnish with toasted almond and served in a saucer glass cold.
