Puerto Rican Monkey Shot is delectable full strength cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Shaken with 20ml rum 151 proof and 20ml banana liqueur with 20ml coffee liqueur and best with an entree or small snack. Just two shots always share a shot then add all ingredients to shaker and shake the hell out of it. Pour all into glass and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
