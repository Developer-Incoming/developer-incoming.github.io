Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Merry Widow
120 ml
Milk Hot
Nutmeg
5% Alcohol By Volume
10 Proof
0.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Warm
  1. Heat glass.
  2. Heat milk.
  3. WARNING Do not boil.
  4. Add coffee liqueur to glass.
  5. Pour in warm milk.
  6. Garnish with nutmeg.
  7. Delicious
Served
Stirred
Warm
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Sweet Dreams is delicious light cocktail at 0.6 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee liqueur merry widow and 120ml milk hot and taken as nightcap before bedtime. Just heat glass then heat milk and warning do not boil. Add coffee liqueur to glass and pour in warm milk and garnish with nutmeg and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
