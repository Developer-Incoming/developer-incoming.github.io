Last Train is enjoyable full bodied cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee schnapps and 30ml bourbon pinhook with 50ml ginger ale soda as well as 190ml ice and served large chunks of meat. Just add ice to glass then pour in coffee schnapps and add bourbon. Top with ginger ale soda and served in a highball glass cold.
