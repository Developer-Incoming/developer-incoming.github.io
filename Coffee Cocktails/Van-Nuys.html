Van Nuys is luscious robust cocktail at 2 standard drinks. Shaken with 45ml tequila primero lunazul and 15ml coffee liqueur applewood with 20ml orange liqueur as well as 10ml lemon juice including 10ml lime juice and good with afternoon tea. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
