Ingredients
30 ml
Rum Dark
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Tia Maria
30 ml
Coffee Brew
10 ml
Cream Whipped
290 ml
Ice
19% Alcohol By Volume
38 Proof
1.5 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Make and chill coffee.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker, less whipped cream.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  7. Garnish with whipped cream.
  8. Pleasant Tasting
Served
Shaken
Cold
Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Jamaican Iced Coffee is pleasant tasting strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml rum dark and 30ml coffee liqueur tia maria with 30ml coffee brew as well as 10ml cream whipped including 290ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just make and chill coffee then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker, less whipped cream. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and garnish with whipped cream and served in a highball glass cold.
