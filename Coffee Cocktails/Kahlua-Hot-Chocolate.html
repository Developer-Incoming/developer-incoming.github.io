Kahlua Hot Chocolate is very enjoyable watered down cocktail at 0.5 standard drinks. Built with 15ml coffee liqueur kahlua and 15ml chocolate liqueur brown gabriel boudier with 10ml chocolate powder as well as 250ml milk hot and taken as nightcap before bedtime. Just heat glass then heat all ingredients, less shavings and warning do not boil. Pour into glass and garnish with shavings and served in a coffee glass warm.
