Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to glass.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass over ice.
  6. Garnish with chocolate syrup.
  7. Palatable

Iced Frappuccino is palatable weak cocktail at 0.5 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml pecan liqueur and 30ml coffee brew with 30ml milk as well as 10ml sugar syrup including 190ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add ice to glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and garnish with chocolate syrup and served in a highball glass cold.
