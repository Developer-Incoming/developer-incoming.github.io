15 ml

60 ml
Milk
100 ml
Ice
Doughnut Licorice
10% Alcohol By Volume
20 Proof
0.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake to mix.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Try it with a licorice flavor doughnut.
  7. Beguiling
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Dawn Buster is beguiling standard cocktail at 0.7 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml coffee liqueur dekuyper and 15ml vanilla liqueur chamarel with 60ml milk as well as 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour all into glass and fill glass with ice and try it with a licorice flavor doughnut and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
