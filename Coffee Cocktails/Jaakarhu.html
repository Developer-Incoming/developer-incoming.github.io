Ingredients
15 ml
Rum Reserva Angostura
15 ml
Brandy St Remy
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Mokatika
100 ml
Ice
30% Alcohol By Volume
60 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Pack ice into glass.
  2. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  3. Stir well.
  4. Pour into glass over ice.
  5. Extremely Pleasant

Jaakarhu: Finnish for polar bear

Served
Stirred
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Jaakarhu is extremely pleasant very strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml rum reserva angostura and 15ml brandy st remy with 30ml coffee liqueur mokatika as well as 100ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just pack ice into glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Pour into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
