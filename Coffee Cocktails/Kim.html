Kim is gorgeous strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml bourbon 1792 sweet wheat and 15ml coffee liqueur mr black amaro with 15ml chocolate liqueur as well as 20ml condensed milk including 30ml cream also 100ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
