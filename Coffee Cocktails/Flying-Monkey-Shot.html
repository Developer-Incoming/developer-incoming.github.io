Flying Monkey Shot is prijatelny quite strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur and 20ml banana liqueur with 20ml whisky cream and best with lunch. Just two shots always share a shot then place coffee liqueur into bottom of glass and layer in banana liqueur. Float whiskey cream on top and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
