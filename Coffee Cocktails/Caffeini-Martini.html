Ingredients
30 ml

15 ml

15 ml

30 ml

100 ml
Ice

Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Place ice into mixing glass.
  2. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  3. Stir well.

Caffeini Martini is bracing full bodied cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee schnapps vizor and 15ml butterscotch liqueur wu tang clan with 15ml chocolate liqueur cream besos de oro as well as 30ml milk including 100ml ice and served with sweet dishes. Just place ice into mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and garnish with brown dust and served in a martini glass cold.
