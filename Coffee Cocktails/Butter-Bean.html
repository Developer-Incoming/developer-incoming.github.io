30 ml

50 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice

1.2 Standard Drinks
Method - Stirred - Cold
  1. Add ice to mixing glass.
  2. Add ingredients to mixing glass.
  3. Stir well.

  5. Garnish with vanilla bean.
  6. Succulent

Martini Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Butter Bean is succulent full bodied cocktail at 1.2 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml coffee liqueur hellfire and 30ml butterscotch liqueur bols with 50ml cream as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and garnish with vanilla bean and served in a martini glass cold.
