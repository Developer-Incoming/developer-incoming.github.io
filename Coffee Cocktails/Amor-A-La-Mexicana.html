Amor A La Mexicana is inspiriting full bodied cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur and 15ml coffee liqueur cazcabel with 15ml vanilla liqueur bols as well as 10ml sugar syrup including 30ml milk also 190ml ice and served for or with tiffins. Just add ice to glass then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.


Milk is a white liquid produced by the mammary glands of mammals. It is the primary source of nutrition for infant mammals before they are able to digest other types of foods.
