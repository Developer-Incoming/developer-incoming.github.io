15 ml

15 ml

15 ml

100 ml
Ice
Grenadine
Chocolate Powder
15% Alcohol By Volume
30 Proof

Method - Layered - Cold
  1. Add ice to mixing glass.
  2. Add ingredients to mixing glass, less grenadine and powder.
  3. Stir well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Float cream on top.
  6. Garnish with grenadine and chocolate.
  7. Delectable

120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Tootsie Roll is delectable full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Layered with 15ml coffee liqueur galliano and 15ml creme de cacao brown with 15ml raspberry liqueur as well as 15ml whisky cream including 30ml cream also 100ml ice and served with sweet dishes. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass, less grenadine and powder and stir well. Strain into glass and float cream on top and garnish with grenadine and chocolate and served in a martini glass cold.
