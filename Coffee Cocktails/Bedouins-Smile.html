Bedouins Smile is mouth watering quite strong cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml krambambula magnificent and 30ml vodka vigor with 40ml coffee brew as well as 10ml lemon juice including 100ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just brew and chill coffee then place ice into mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Stir well and strain into glass and served in a martini glass cold.
