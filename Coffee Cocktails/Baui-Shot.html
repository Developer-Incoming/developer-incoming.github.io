Baui Shot is revitalizing robust cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 20ml coffee liqueur wild wombat and 20ml peppermint schnapps mcguinness with 20ml cape velvet cream liqueur and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just two shots always share a shot then add all ingredients to shaker and quick dry shake without ice. Pour into glass and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
