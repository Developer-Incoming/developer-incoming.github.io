Ingredients
30 ml
Cognac Merlet Exclusive
15 ml
Amaretto Liqueur Soiree
195 ml
Coffee Brew
10 ml
Sugar Syrup
Cream Whipped
7% Alcohol By Volume
14 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Brew coffee.
  3. Add cognac to glass.
  4. Pour in amaretto liqueur.
  5. Add sugar syrup.
  6. Pour in coffee.
  7. Stir a little.
  8. Optimal service temperature would be 60C to 70C or 140F to 158F.
  9. Top with whipped cream.
  10. Moreish
Served
Poured
Warm
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Midnight Express is moreish mild cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml cognac merlet exclusive and 15ml amaretto liqueur soiree with 195ml coffee brew as well as 10ml sugar syrup and best when a coffee is needed. Just warm glass then brew coffee and add cognac to glass. Pour in amaretto liqueur and add sugar syrup and pour in coffee then stir a little and optimal service temperature would be 60c to 70c or 140f to 158f and top with whipped cream and served in a coffee glass warm.
