Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Trader Vics
30 ml
Coffee Brew
30 ml
Cream
Pastry Danish Cherry
9% Alcohol By Volume
18 Proof
0.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Room Temperature
  1. Brew coffee.
  2. Warm glass.
  3. Pour brewed coffee into warm glass.
  4. Float coffee liqueur on top.
  5. Float pouring cream on top.
  6. Good with a cherry danish pastry.
  7. Restoring
Served
Layered
Room Temperature
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Cafe Creme is restoring medium cocktail at 0.6 standard drinks. Layered with 30ml coffee liqueur trader vics and 30ml coffee brew with 30ml cream and best when a coffee is needed. Just brew coffee then warm glass and pour brewed coffee into warm glass. Float coffee liqueur on top and float pouring cream on top and good with a cherry danish pastry and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
