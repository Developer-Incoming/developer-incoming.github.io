White African is glorious strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Built with 30ml vodka pono and 30ml coffee liqueur walders with 30ml coconut cream as well as 100ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just fill glass with ice cubes then pour in vodka and add coffee liqueur. Pour in coconut cream and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
