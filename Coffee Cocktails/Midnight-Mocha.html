Ingredients
25 ml
Coffee Liqueur Louisas
10 ml
Creme de Cacao Brown
25 ml
Vodka Bols
10 ml
Chocolate Syrup
100 ml
Ice
Cream Whipped
Cinnamon
24% Alcohol By Volume
48 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Ribbon syrup inside of glass.
  2. Place ice into shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker, less cream and cinnamon.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Garnish with a quenelle of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon.
  7. Inviting
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Midnight Mocha is inviting robust cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 25ml coffee liqueur louisas and 10ml creme de cacao brown with 25ml vodka bols as well as 10ml chocolate syrup including 100ml ice and served with sweet dishes. Just ribbon syrup inside of glass then place ice into shaker and add all ingredients to shaker, less cream and cinnamon. Shake well and strain into glass and garnish with a quenelle of whipped cream and a sprinkle of cinnamon and served in a saucer glass cold.
