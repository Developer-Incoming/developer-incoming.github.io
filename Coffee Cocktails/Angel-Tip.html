Ingredients
30 ml
Amaretto Liqueur My Amaretto
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Quick Brown Fox
30 ml
Cream
100 ml
Ice
Truffle Chocolate Coffee
16% Alcohol By Volume
32 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake to mix.
  4. Pour all into glass.
  5. Fill glass with ice.
  6. Garnish with a coffee truffle chocolate.
  7. Luscious
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl
