Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Sierra
30 ml
Ice Cream Coffee
30 ml
Vanilla Liqueur
30 ml
Ice Cream Vanilla
Praline
15% Alcohol By Volume
30 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Add scoop of coffee ice cream to glass.
  2. Add scoop of vanilla ice cream to glass.
  3. Pour coffee liqueur over coffee ice cream.
  4. Pour vanilla liqueur over vanilla ice cream.
  5. Sprinkle praline over all.
  6. Very Palatable
Served
Poured
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Madrilene Bombe is very palatable full bodied cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur sierra and 30ml ice cream coffee with 30ml vanilla liqueur as well as 30ml ice cream vanilla and best with lunch. Just add scoop of coffee ice cream to glass then add scoop of vanilla ice cream to glass and pour coffee liqueur over coffee ice cream. Pour vanilla liqueur over vanilla ice cream and sprinkle praline over all and served in a saucer glass cold.
