Voodoo is rejuvenating standard cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Stirred with 15ml butterscotch liqueur and 15ml coconut liqueur tamborine mountain with 15ml coffee liqueur scylla as well as 55ml milk including 190ml ice and best with buffet style food. Just pack ice into glass then add all ingredients to shaker and quick dry shake without ice. Pour into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
