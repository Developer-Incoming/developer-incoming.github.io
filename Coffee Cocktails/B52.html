B52 is yummy digestive strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur gabriel boudier and 20ml whisky cream with 20ml orange liqueur marie brizard and best with lunch. Just add coffee liqueur then float whisky cream and float orange liqueur on top and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
