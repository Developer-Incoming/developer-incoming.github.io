Ingredients
15 ml
Rum Dark Pussers Black
15 ml
Rum Light White Diplomatico
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Cayman Reef
15 ml
Lime Juice
100 ml
Ice
30% Alcohol By Volume
60 Proof
1.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Quick shake to mix.
  4. Pour everything into glass.
  5. Top glass with ice.
  6. Refreshing
Served
Shaken
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Fat Sailor is refreshing very strong cocktail at 1.4 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml rum dark pussers black and 15ml rum light white diplomatico with 15ml coffee liqueur cayman reef as well as 15ml lime juice including 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and quick shake to mix. Pour everything into glass and top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
