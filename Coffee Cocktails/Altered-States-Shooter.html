Altered States Shooter is delightful strong cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur tia maria dark and 20ml peach liqueur bols with 20ml whisky cream and good with afternoon tea. Just two shots always share a shot then pour coffee into bottom of glass and float peach liqueur. Float whisky cream and served in a shot glass at room temperature.
