Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Decaf Richardos
30 ml
Cognac
10 ml
Licor 43
20 ml
Condensed Milk
Heat Cubes
16% Alcohol By Volume
32 Proof
1.1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Warm
  1. Warm glass.
  2. Warm shaker and heat cubes.
  3. Add all ingredients to warm shaker.
  4. Vigorously shake until warm.
  5. Fine strain into glass.
  6. Apetitoso
Served
Shaken
Warm
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Asiatico is apetitoso full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur decaf richardos and 30ml cognac with 10ml licor 43 as well as 20ml condensed milk and best with lunch. Just warm glass then warm shaker and heat cubes and add all ingredients to warm shaker. Vigorously shake until warm and fine strain into glass and served in a sherry glass warm.
