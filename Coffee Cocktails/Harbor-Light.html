Ingredients
20 ml
Coffee Liqueur Trader Vics
20 ml
Orange Liqueur
20 ml
Cognac
10 ml
Rum 151 Proof Lemon Hart and Son
35% Alcohol By Volume
70 Proof
1.9 Standard Drinks
Method - Layered - Room Temperature
  1. Add coffee liqueur into bottom of glass.
  2. Spoon orange liqueur.
  3. Add cognac as a layer.
  4. Float rum on top.
  5. Delish
Served
Layered
Room Temperature
Sherry Glass
90ml, 3oz, 9cl

Harbor Light is delish heavy cocktail at 1.9 standard drinks. Layered with 20ml coffee liqueur trader vics and 20ml orange liqueur with 20ml cognac as well as 10ml rum 151 proof lemon hart and son and best with lunch. Just add coffee liqueur into bottom of glass then spoon orange liqueur and add cognac as a layer. Float rum on top and served in a sherry glass at room temperature.
