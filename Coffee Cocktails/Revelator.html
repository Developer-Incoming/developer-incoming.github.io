Revelator is comestible full strength cocktail at 2.6 standard drinks. Stirred with 60ml tequila blanco mucha liga and 30ml amaro averna and best with buffet style food. Just add ingredients to mixing glass then gently stir and strain into glass and garnish with roasted coffee beans and served in a martini glass at room temperature.
