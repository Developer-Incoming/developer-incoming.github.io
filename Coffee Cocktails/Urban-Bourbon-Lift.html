Ingredients
45 ml
Coffee Liqueur Cream Somrus
15 ml
Bourbon
10 ml
Orgeat Syrup
30 ml
Soda Water
90 ml
Ice
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Add half the ice to shaker.
  2. Pour in liqueur and bourbon.
  3. Add syrup.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Pour all into glass.
  6. Add more ice.
  7. Top with soda water.
  8. Beguiling
Served
Poured
Cold
Old Fashioned Glass
200ml, 7oz, 20cl

Urban Bourbon Lift is beguiling average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 45ml coffee liqueur cream somrus and 15ml bourbon with 10ml orgeat syrup as well as 30ml soda water including 90ml ice and best with lunch. Just add half the ice to shaker then pour in liqueur and bourbon and add syrup. Shake well and pour all into glass and add more ice then top with soda water and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
