Ingredients
60 ml
Hazelnut Liqueur Soplica
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Leopold Bros
20 ml
Pomegranate Syrup
100 ml
Ice
22% Alcohol By Volume
44 Proof
1.6 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Add ice to shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker, less coffee liqueur.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Pour coffee liqueur into glass.
  6. Divine

Vixen: a spirited or quarrelsome woman

Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Vixen is divine quite strong cocktail at 1.6 standard drinks. Shaken with 60ml hazelnut liqueur soplica and 15ml coffee liqueur leopold bros with 20ml pomegranate syrup as well as 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker, less coffee liqueur and shake well. Strain into glass and pour coffee liqueur into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
