Ingredients
15 ml
Whisky Cream Irish Baileys
15 ml
Coffee Liqueur Kahlua
60 ml
Ice Cream Vanilla
Milo Powder
6% Alcohol By Volume
12 Proof
0.4 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Chill glass.
  2. Add scoop if ice cream to glass.
  3. Pour over coffee liqueur.
  4. Add in whisky cream.
  5. Sprinkle milo on top.
  6. Delectable
Served
Poured
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Milo Ice Cream is delectable weak cocktail at 0.4 standard drinks. Built with 15ml whisky cream irish baileys and 15ml coffee liqueur kahlua with 60ml ice cream vanilla and best with dessert type dishes. Just chill glass then add scoop if ice cream to glass and pour over coffee liqueur. Add in whisky cream and sprinkle milo on top and served in a saucer glass cold.
