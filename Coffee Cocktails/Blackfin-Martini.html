Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Liqueur Bottega
30 ml
Vodka Manly Spirits
30 ml
Coffee Brew
100 ml
Ice
19% Alcohol By Volume
38 Proof
1.3 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Place ice into shaker.
  2. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Strain into glass.
  5. Schmackhaft
Served
Shaken
Cold
Saucer Glass
120ml, 5oz, 12cl

Blackfin Martini is schmackhaft strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml coffee liqueur bottega and 30ml vodka manly spirits with 30ml coffee brew as well as 100ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just place ice into shaker then add all ingredients to shaker and shake well. Strain into glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
