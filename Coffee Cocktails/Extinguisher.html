Extinguisher is scrumptious medium cocktail at 2.1 standard drinks. Built with 10ml vodka and 10ml gin karu with 10ml rum as well as 10ml orange liqueur including 10ml coffee liqueur also 10ml lemon juice together with 30ml cranberry juice and 200ml beer and best at tea time. Just use nice cold glass then pour all all spirits and add lemon juice to glass. Pour in nip of cranberry juice and top glass with beer and served in a highball glass cold.
