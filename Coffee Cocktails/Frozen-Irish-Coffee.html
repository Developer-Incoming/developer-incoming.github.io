Ingredients
30 ml
Whiskey Irish
50 ml
Coffee Brew
60 ml
Ice Cream Vanilla
100 ml
Ice
10 ml
Sugar Syrup
8% Alcohol By Volume
16 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Shaken - Cold
  1. Make coffee and chill until cold.
  2. Add ice to shaker.
  3. Add all ingredients to shaker, less ice cream.
  4. Shake well.
  5. Strain into glass.
  6. Garnish with a scoop of ice cream.
  7. Blassome
Served
Shaken
Cold
Coffee Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Frozen Irish Coffee is blassome smooth cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml whiskey irish and 50ml coffee brew with 60ml ice cream vanilla as well as 100ml ice including 10ml sugar syrup and best with dessert type dishes. Just make coffee and chill until cold then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker, less ice cream. Shake well and strain into glass and garnish with a scoop of ice cream and served in a coffee glass cold.
