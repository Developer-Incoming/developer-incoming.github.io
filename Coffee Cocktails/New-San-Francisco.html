New San Francisco is scrummy strong cocktail at 1.5 standard drinks. Stirred with 30ml cherry liqueur and 30ml vodka with 10ml lime juice as well as 10ml grenadine including 30ml coffee brew also 100ml ice and best with lunch. Just brew and chill coffee then add ice to mixing glass and add ingredients to mixing glass. Stir well and strain into glass and garnish with maraschino cherry and served in a saucer glass cold.
