Blind Russian is restoring average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Shaken with 15ml butterscotch liqueur grove and 15ml coffee liqueur with 15ml whisky cream hellyers rd as well as 15ml chocolate liqueur brown vok including 50ml milk also 290ml ice and best served with farinaceous starchy dishes. Just add ice to high ball glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a highball glass cold.
