Served

Highball Glass
300ml, 10.5oz, 30cl

Canecutter is inviting and strawless standard cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Built with 15ml rum and 15ml coffee liqueur hellfire with 70ml cola soda as well as 190ml ice and good with roasted or barbecue foods. Just fill glass with ice then add rum to glass and add coffee liqueur. Top with cola soda and served in a highball glass cold.
