Stem Winder is bewitching very strong cocktail at 1.8 standard drinks. Shaken with 20ml orange liqueur and 20ml port with 20ml creme de cacao brown as well as 10ml coffee liqueur jagermeister including 10ml rum 151 proof anns cove also 200ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to glass then add ice to shaker and add all ingredients to shaker. Shake well and strain into glass over ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
