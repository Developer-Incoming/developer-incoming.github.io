Vanilla Hazelnut Espresso is mouth watering strong cocktail at 1.3 standard drinks. Shaken with 30ml vodka coffee jumping goat and 15ml coffee liqueur with 15ml almond liqueur as well as 10ml vanilla syrup including 20ml coffee also 100ml ice and served before a meal to stimulate appetite. Just add ice to mixing glass then add ingredients to mixing glass and stir well. Strain into glass and served in a martini glass cold.
