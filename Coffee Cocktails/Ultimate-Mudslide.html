Ultimate Mudslide is glorious full bodied cocktail at 1.1 standard drinks. Blended with 15ml vodka and 15ml coffee liqueur cazcabel with 15ml whisky cream as well as 15ml banana pulp including 15ml milk also 15ml cream together with 30ml ice and good with afternoon tea. Just put ice into blender then add all ingredients to blender and blend until silky smooth. Pour blended mixture into glass and garnish with chocolate shavings and served in a saucer glass cold.
