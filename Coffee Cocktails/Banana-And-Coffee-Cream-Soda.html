Ingredients
30 ml
Coffee Cream Liqueur Royal Rhino
10 ml
Rum Dark
10 ml
Banana Liqueur Baitz
10 ml
Lime Juice
30 ml
Cream
30 ml
Banana Pulp
30 ml
Soda Water
90 ml
Ice
7% Alcohol By Volume
14 Proof
0.8 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Add ice to blender.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender, less soda water.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into glass.
  5. Top with soda water
  6. Garnish with banana.
  7. Enjoyable

Blended
Cold

250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Banana And Coffee Cream Soda is enjoyable mild cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Blended with 30ml coffee cream liqueur royal rhino and 10ml rum dark with 10ml banana liqueur baitz as well as 10ml lime juice including 30ml cream also 30ml banana pulp together with 30ml soda water and 90ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender, less soda water and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and top with soda water and garnish with banana and served in a poco grande glass cold.
