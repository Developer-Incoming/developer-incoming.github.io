Jagermeister
60 ml
Coffee Brew
100 ml
Ice
12% Alcohol By Volume
24 Proof
1 Standard Drinks
Method - Poured - Cold
  1. Brew coffee and chill.
  2. Use cold glass.
  3. Add half the ice to shaker.
  4. Add all ingredients to shaker.
  5. Shake well.
  6. Pour everything into glass.
  7. Top glass with ice.

Jagermeister Cold Brew is relacionan average cocktail at 1 standard drinks. Built with 30ml jagermeister and 60ml coffee brew with 100ml ice and best when a coffee is needed. Just brew coffee and chill then use cold glass and add half the ice to shaker. Add all ingredients to shaker and shake well and pour everything into glass then top glass with ice and served in a old fashioned glass cold.
