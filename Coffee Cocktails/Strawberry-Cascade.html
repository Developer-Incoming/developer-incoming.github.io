Ingredients
40 ml
Vodka Kavka Polish
10 ml
Galliano
10 ml
Coffee Liqueur Lawrenny
60 ml
Strawberry Pulp
30 ml
Cream
90 ml
Ice
14% Alcohol By Volume
28 Proof
1.7 Standard Drinks
Method - Blended - Cold
  1. Add ice to blender.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender, less coffee liqueur.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into glass.
  5. Float coffee liqueur on top.
  6. Palatable
Served
Blended
Cold
Poco Grande Glass
250ml, 8oz, 25cl

Strawberry Cascade is palatable full bodied cocktail at 1.7 standard drinks. Blended with 40ml vodka kavka polish and 10ml galliano with 10ml coffee liqueur lawrenny as well as 60ml strawberry pulp including 30ml cream also 90ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender, less coffee liqueur and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and float coffee liqueur on top and served in a poco grande glass cold.
