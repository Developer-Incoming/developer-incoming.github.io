  2. Heat glass.
  3. Add coffee liqueur to glass.

Fire And Forget is beguiling mild cocktail at 0.8 standard drinks. Built with 30ml coffee liqueur sutherland and 30ml apple schnapps charles regnier with 90ml coffee brew and best with lunch. Just brew black coffee then heat glass and add coffee liqueur to glass. Pour in ounce of apple liqueur and stir in brewed coffee and good with a stuffed doughnut and served in a old fashioned glass warm.
