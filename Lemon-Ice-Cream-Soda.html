Lemon Ice Cream Soda is scrumptious spider watery mocktail with no alcohol. Blended with 30ml lemon juice and 10ml sugar syrup with 30ml soda water as well as 50ml ice cream vanilla including 120ml ice and best with lunch. Just add ice to blender then add all ingredients to blender, less soda and blend until smooth. Pour into glass and top with soda and served in a poco grande glass cold.
