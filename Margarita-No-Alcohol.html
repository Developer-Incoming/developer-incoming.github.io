Margarita No Alcohol is luscious watery mocktail with no alcohol. Shaken with 30ml lime juice and 30ml lime syrup with 30ml non alcoholic spirit as well as 10ml sugar syrup including 100ml ice and best served with fish and seafood dishes. Just add ice to shaker then add all ingredients to shaker, less salt and salt rim of glass. Shake well and strain into salt rimed glass and served in a saucer glass cold.
